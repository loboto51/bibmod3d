This is part of my ongoing digital kitbashing to make some post-apocalypse figures wargaming. He is part of a construction worker/junkyard faction. More figures to follow. The size comparison figure, Danny, is also available to print. 

As you can see from the pictures above he prints up a little short, being based on a dwarf body after all, but scales well with other figures. 

I printed this on my Anycubic Mono SE, and the basic Chuitubox supports worked just fine.
