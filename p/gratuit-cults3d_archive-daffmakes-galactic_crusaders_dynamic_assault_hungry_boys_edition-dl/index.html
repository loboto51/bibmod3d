How hungry are they?
So hungry they could eat an entire planet.

Variety of chainaxe wielding maniacs in segmented armour pattern.
Comes with regular helm, Sarum helm, and Hungry Boys Veteran helm for all pose types.

Extra variety can be gotten by mirroring the various poses. In particular with the dual and two-handed chainaxe variants.

For the flying marines, they have holes in the jump pack to accept a 1mm rod as a flight stand. For my example (see my generic assault marines), I cut halfway along the curve of a paperclip, inserted the curved end into the hole with a bit of superglue and adjusted the tilt of the marine from there. Then, drill a hole into your base of choice, and adjust the height of the paperclip as desired.

There is also a "_standoffs" version. This is something I'm playing around with where the models' feet are offset from the plug, allowing you to use the plug as a stand when painting, and then slice or snip off the small cylinders to allow you to glue the feet directly onto the base, for a seamless finish.

If you'd rather adjust the placement of the rods yourself for use with straight brass rod, or clear acrylic rods, I've provided the "_nohole" versions for this purpose.
