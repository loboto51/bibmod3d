The Indebted are the main workforce on the 400 moons. Some of them take up arms, either to rebel against their Void Baron overlords, or in their service.

Designed for 6mm/8mm gaming in mind. Compatible with games such as Adeptus Titanicus or Battletech.

You'll save 40% when buying these as a bundle rather than separately.

This package contains the four existing kits:
1) [Armed Indebted (rifles)](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/smallscale-rifle-armed-worker-gang)
2) [Armed Indebted (close combat)](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/smallscale-close-combat-brutes)
3) [Armed Indebted (pistols)](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/smallscale-gunslingers)
4) [Armed Indebted (flamethrowers)](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/smallscale-flame-troopers)
5) [25mm round bases](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/25mm-round-bases-for-6mm-8mm-miniatures-tech-shanty-texture)  (extra inserted bits not included)

All armed gangers are either on 5.8mm round inserts or without such inserts. Note that the 25mm bases are just the bases, not the insert detail bits from the separate kit.
