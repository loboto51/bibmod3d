The Empire of Sentient Man has the diverse army taking part in permament war against the foul traitors and treacherous aliens. Mostly, the army is represented by ill-equipped militia in various ballistic armour (including coats), wielding only laser rifles. Red Militia has such a tragic fate. While being the defenders of their land, they have to reach the planets too far beyond comprehension, test the mud with their boots and treads and hope for weapons to beat unfamiliar threats.

The Red Militia models, with faces diminished and bases added, are shared for free with the permission of NotANiceGuy (https://cults3d.com/en/users/NotANiceGuy/3d-models).

The pack contains:
- command squad including lieutenant, communication officer, standard bearer and sergeant;
- line infantry in 8 different poses;
- heavy weapons teams with heavy machine gun, automatic gun, laser cannon, missile launcher; 
- random loaders and mortar crew;
- plus non-Red Militia 3 commissars and 2 psykers.

UPDATE 2023.08.15:
- added separate heavy weapons;
- improved missile launcher crew, version two added;
- mortars added.

UPDATE 2023.12.14:
- Master of Discipline model added;
- All the Red Militia are downscaled to fit 8mm scale;
- All the weapons on carriages are downscaled;
- New Missile Launcher team for nostalgic feels.

Pictures with resin prints of pre-release version of 8mm Red Militia models included. The current models have sturdier bases, and the standard bearer got thicker banner pole.
