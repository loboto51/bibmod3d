***UPDATE: 2020-08-18***
I uploaded revised models.  After printing and receiving feedback from others, I modified the designs to make the models thicker.  The models should now be sturdier, making it easier to trim away supports and play with the models without ankles and halberd hafts snapping.
*******************************

I remixed a pile of files from Thingiverse, mostly from EvilFrog (whose stuff is great!), in order to make a full regiment of Empire halberdier strips for the game Warmaster.  These are 10mm scale, and would work for late medieval / renaissance troops as well, though nobody fought with a halberd and a shield historically, as a halberd takes two hands to wield.  I also included a 40x20x2mm unit base.  I highly recommend printing these strips in resin, with supports, at 0.025mm layer height.  That's what I plan to do.
