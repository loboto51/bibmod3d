Hi all!

This pack is a set of variants of the Blade Super Heavy Tank. In the pack you get all the parts to print the following:

- Deathblade
- Hellblade
- Shadowblade
- Quakeblade
- Thunderblade

This pack is designed to conform to 6mm scale games, but increase their scale by 33.3% for 8mm scaled games.

In addition, this series of tanks features modular armour and sponson mounts. You get a selection of parts to outfit the sponsons and turrets any way you deem fit, and there are included magnet holes for ease of installation (2x1mm) for the large secondary turrets.

You also get a hatch part that can be installed if you don't want a turret there (perhaps using underslung turrets only?)

Anyway, enjoy, thanks for reading :)
