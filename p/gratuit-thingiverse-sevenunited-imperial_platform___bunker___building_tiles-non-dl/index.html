<b>Improved version of my Modular Imperial Building/Platform.</b>

There will be in future more packs to have barricades on the roof, a roof in general, a second floor etc.

The tiles can be glued together or can also be connected via magnets (6x3mm).

As Walkway you can use the prefab pieces or make your own shape. All pieces build a grid of 25mm/multiple of 25mm at the top.  

Instead using the tiles in this pack you could also use the ones from the floor tiles pack:
https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3895156
