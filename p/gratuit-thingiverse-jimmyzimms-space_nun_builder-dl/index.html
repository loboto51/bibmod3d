Builder Doll for anyone wanting to have quality components for making your own Space Nuns Army remixes. Based on files from Rhimlock base parts and normalized the figure and recreated Moonjam weapons from scratch. Contains Celestine weapons, heavy weapons (w/Packs), and jump packs for sisters as well.

Feel free to use for your own epic scale creations.

If you're just wanting completed and posed figures, look at my Cults profile instead.
