Ok, so there are many ways to confiture the Malc, so here are a few of them.  I did variants based on the combinations of the mainguns (Vanquisher, Battle Cannon, and Double LasCans with 3 different hull guns (lascanon, HvyBolter, Demolisher) and the 2 different sponsons of Autocannons and LasCans. 

These STLs have been painstakingly repaired/ filled and then had the underneath scooped out to reduce suction. 

Now, if the one you are seeking doesn't quite exist, just open them into 3D builder and cut/paste to your hearts desire.  Its pretty easy - plenty of tutorials on the tube of YOU.

These models are all ultimately based on Danny_Cyanide's work.  They were reduced in scale and assembled by Korgaan, but had a lot of internal hollow (especially around the sides/ tracks) that needed to be filled.  I did a ton of repair work.  Other creators have worked on these prior, but I noticed there were still internal hollows, so the ones here should be a better print with a lower chance of resin leakage - it is difficult to take multi-part STLs and assemble a single print model without some stuff missed - so this is not in any way a criticism of those fine works.

There are 8 tanks here:
BattleTurret with AutoCannonSpn and HvyBolterHull
BattleTurret with LasCannonSpn and HvyBolterHull
BattleTurret with LasCannonSpn and DemolisherHull
DblLasCaneTurret with LasCannonSpn and DemolisherHull
DblLasCaneTurret with LasCannonSpn and HvyBolterHull
DblLasCaneTurret with LasCannonSpn and LascanonHull
VanqTurret with LasCannonSpn and DemolisherHull
VanqTurret with LasCannonSpn and LascanonHull
