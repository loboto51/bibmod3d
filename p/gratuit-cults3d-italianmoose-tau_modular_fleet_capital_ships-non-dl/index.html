All the bits to make the old-school Tau fleet capital ships from the classic tabletop game Battlefleet Gothic. Lots of bits and complete models to create Explorer, Hero, and Merchant class vessels. Also a cargo module, and a blank module back to create your own. There is a hole for a stem in each spine section, drill through the circle in the bottom.
