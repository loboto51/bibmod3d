These menacing multi-legged minions come in a variety of scales, as originally sculpted, but their universal nature and lack of scale cues enables them to be used as massed armies in nearly any setting, and can be exchanged easily between settings.  A 28mm spiderdrone looks impressive next to a 15mm human, while the 6mm spiderbots look like they can crawl over and pull down a 28mm figure.

The 28mm, multi-part versions are crafted in separate legs and blasters set at multiple angles.  The 15mm and 6mm versions are tiny and fused.  You can fill entire print beds with the little creepy-crawlers!

Originally created as escort bots and onboard infantry for the ScorpionMech, which I also offer, these little bots come in deployed and stowed versions, with full and empty carrier racks that can be adapted to other builds.

ScorpionMechs and other figures are shown for reference, and are sold separately.
