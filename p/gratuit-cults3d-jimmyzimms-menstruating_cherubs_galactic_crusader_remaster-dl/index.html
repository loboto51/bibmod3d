***THIS IS A WORK IN PROGRESS***

Tiny troops which are perfect for giving your games of epic conflict a sense of epic scale. And what can be more perfect than the golden perfect winged assault cherubs?

Building on the foundation that Moonjam left us, I've remodeled and rebuilt most of the base Galactic Crusader parts into higher detailed versions that will allow those of you with 8k and 12k printers to showcase your models with. In addition, the figures are easily to print at 200% (maybe even 400%?) for use with other games systems and scales.

The blender source is included containing my hobo-rigged figures and pose library. I wasn't feeling all too well and took some liberties/shortcuts than I would for a more generic release but should get you most of the way even when I used basic object parenting for a few parts. Sorry =/

Figures have been run through 3DBuilder to merge and decimate the meshes to reasonable levels though you can export your own from the Blend. I've also uploaded a zip of just the unmerged undecimated figures for your further use if so desired. Not that plinths, risers, and banner icons are NOT merged so you can rework or replace stuff as works for you.

Big shouts out to Moonjam for the base content to build on. Icons from Lord Chronos. Heads based on work from M3rcurymordbid.

If you like what I did and want to leave a tip it would be appreciated.
