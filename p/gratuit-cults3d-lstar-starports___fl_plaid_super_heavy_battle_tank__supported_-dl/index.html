A humble reworking of Woddish's excellent design, optimised for easy printing.

Supported and tweaked for easy printing, including:
- Slots for 5mm magnets for anyone who like rotating turrets and making pew pew noises, a masterfully crafted 5mm cylinder for anyone wanting to glue the turrets in place
- Command options, and a remixable hatch
- Scooped hull, reducing resin use by ~20%
- Lychee Scenes for anyone wanting to tweak supports/rafts/hollowing

*HOLD UP! You forgot to upload the commanders and sponson weapons? What's the deal!?*
I'm going to create separate packs for hatch options and sponson weapons so that I can update them in one central place as I add new poses, armour marks, weapon options, etc. For now, you can find the sponson weapons in the Spar-10 pack, and commanders in the Pred-8R pack.
