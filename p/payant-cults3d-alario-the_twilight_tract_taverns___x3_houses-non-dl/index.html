These carefully designed models have been created specifically for 3D printing and are ideal for board games, dioramas and lovers of historical architecture. Each house is tested and ready to print on FDM and SLA printers, ensuring excellent quality and detail at all levels.

The Twilight Tract Taverns project is a magical world in your hands!

Victoria is a 3D artist, and Victoria lovingly creates unique models for 3D printing. This project is the result of her passion for the fantasy world and attention to detail.

All models are designed specifically for high-quality 3D printing: with clean parts, convenient assembly and optimized support.
