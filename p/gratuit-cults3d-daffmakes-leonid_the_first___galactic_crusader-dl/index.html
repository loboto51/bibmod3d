Glory To The First!

Leader and Father of the Gloomy Knights. Useful for a diorama, or as part of a command squad.

Comes in four poses!

Considering this model is bigger than the usual 8mm infantry, He comes on a set of standoffs - the model can be painted, then cut off the standoffs to be glued to a base.

Thanks to Moonjammy for the base models, and the DA Symbol is from Bumfluff.
