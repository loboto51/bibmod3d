Rumbling forward on heavy tracks, the tanks of the Imperial Auxilliary forces are capable of decimating enemy forces with overwhelming firepower. This heavier variant of tank was specifically designed for assaulting trench networks, able to cross gaps their smaller brethren would fall into.

This is a sample file of a set of 25 STLS, that features two hull variants - One with inset sponsons, and one with external sponsons.

The main set has been made to be complimentary with 8mm scale battle tanks of a recently released 8mm grim-dark future wargame.

Please see my other items for a free sample of this set, and a range of other 8mm scale and other wargames and hobby models. I am an enthusiastic amateur designer, self and video-tutorial taught, so am always open to constructive feedback.
