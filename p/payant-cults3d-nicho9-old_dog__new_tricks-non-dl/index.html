This Old Dog is a remix of a mono pose model found on Thingiverse. I've separated the weapons and added a few more I've found around the web.

It's been scaled to suit AT/ LI proportions

There's a standing leg pose, 2 different running poses and a selection of weapons available.

I've added magnet holes suitable for 3mm W x 2mm D magnets in the weapons and the torso.

There is also a preset plate of 3 Old Dogs suitable for Elegoo Saturn (4k) and larger plates.

Update: 

I've added 3 80mm bases both supported and unsupported
