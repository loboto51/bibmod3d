This is a scaled down and modified 6-8mm version of [Landon73-LeesedRenfort's](https://www.thingiverse.com/Landon73-LeesedRenfort/about) excellent Inquisitor K Man and His Party Throne, and uploaded with his permission.

Please visit [https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/morty-nun-bot-leesedrenfort](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/morty-nun-bot-leesedrenfort) and grab the original for a much better 28mm version.

This epic scale remix has various fills, detail removals, thickenings, etc. to make it printable at small scale. I have also removed the banner poles and added guides for replacement wire poles on the banners. I use 0.3mm piano wire. I optimised the files as much as I could, but Meshmixer wasn't playing nice.

Not a model for the faint of heart, but totally awesome if you like tiny things.
