With Orktober looming i wanted to print some of the Jazzmantis retro space orcs but needed additional weapon options so i put together this remix for them as shown in the photo's above. However, this post only contains the remixed weapon arms and users will need to download the wide assortment of bodies/heads that Jazzmantis offerers from the links below.

Modular kit includes rifles, carbines, shotguns, pistols, melee weapons, grenades, and various special weapons.

Note: users will need to mirror some arms and Zip files are used for organisation.

If you enjoy these models please consider [tipping me a coffee](https://www.paypal.me/dotplot1) to encourage creation of new designs and improving existing ones.
