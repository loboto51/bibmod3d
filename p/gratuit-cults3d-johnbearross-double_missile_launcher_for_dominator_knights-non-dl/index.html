I wanted a more "Warlord"-like appearance for my personal Knights, so I came up with this new turret system.

Five parts, supported and clean.

1 separate missile warhead (print multiple).

3 missile launcher bodies with hollow base (I glue 1/2" washers inside to magnetize my models). The round mounting studs in the launch tubes can also be painted up as warheads, if you don't like the separate warhead bit.
Orientations are vertical-angled, horizontal-flat, and horizontal-angled.

1 standard launcher base that should mount to unmagnetized knights with a 2mm plug.
Some fitting may be required.
