Leading their brothers into battle, the chaplains of the Prime Marines in Space wear deaths-head helmets to strike fear into their foes.

Inspired by a recent grim-dark computer game, this studded, skull-faced helm would be an interesting conversion piece for your grim dark future wargame models. I'm working on a number of other comparable heads, so hopefully more packs will follow soon! 

Included are supported and unsupported versions of the file, plus a slightly scaled up version with larger studs.
