Giant boars, much like regular boars, travelled in groups known as "sounders." The number of giant boars in a sounder varied, but oftentimes it totalled up to eight. Unlike ordinary pigs, giant boars were vicious and would attack any creature that approached, typically trying to charge their opponents and gore them with their tusks.

Giant Boar for use in any epic adventure! Compatible with D&D and tabletop role-playing games.

Pre-supported version (for resin printing) in 32mm and 54mm scaling options provided!
