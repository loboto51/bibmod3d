Riding in on the winds of 80s nostalgia like a goofily-muscular phantom, it’s Warduke!

Warduke was an action figure (possibly the best?) from the LJN Advanced Dungeons & Dragons action figures (funny to me that they bothered with the "Advanced" part) released in the mid-80s.

Warduke prints with minimal supports and is in the current industry-standard 32mm scale (compatible with major ranges such as Reaper, Games Workshop, CMON, et cetera).
