When you are in need of long-distance haulage, you need a lorry. Need some protection for your convoy? Try these new armed battle lorries.

The perfect addition to your Y'Orkshire haulage business.

This is a full remix of Vohvel's model, found here:  https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:4862377

This model is intended to act as a proxy for epic Sci-Fi games. I do not recommend resizing up to heroic scale as the detail will be fairly poor.

This model is free to download and print. I have no intention to profit from this model as it is not 100% my creation. However, if you appreciate my work and want to leave a tip, you can visit my Ko-Fi page: https://ko-fi.com/stuffbycwen

This model contains various items from multiple sources (in true Y'orcist fashion) in addition to tweaks, additions and simplifications made by myself.
