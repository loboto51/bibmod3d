Tired of seeing the enemy aircrafts hunting your troops unchallenged?? We have the solution.
Afraid of enemy long range units shooting down your BTS?? We have the solution.
Worried about that big hordes running towards you?? Yes, WE HAVE THE SOLUTION.

Here you can find 3 wonderful "solutions": anti air tank, missile launcher tank and mortar tank (note that this one is modelled after the newest version, not the classic one).

They all share the same chasis thanks to STC magic and the same turret thanks to MkHand Industries amazing work. Simply print one chasis, one turret and a pair of mirrored weapon options for the tanks you desire, or mix and match then for fun results xD.

As always, I included both supported and unsupported versions.

This is a remix with the following parts: 
Track guards: https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3647995
Skull insignia: https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:399182
Body, turret and weapons by MkHand Industries: https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/epic-scale-anti-air-artillery

UPDATE: Added a ZIP with all files in case Thingiverse does its magic again...
Apparently clicking the download all button leads to the 404 page, simply go to thing files tab and download the zip file, simple workaround for a buggy site.

UPDATE: Test printed nicely, except for the missiles that printed kind of weird, I resupported them and reuploaded the fixed files (also updated the ZIP file).

UPDATE 15/Dec/2020: The antiair cannons were too long for my like, so I added 2 more versions closer to the 40k model proportion, so now you can choose xD (also updated the ZIP file).

UPDATE 15/01/2021: Added options with 0 and 1 missiles left (so you can have all possible configurations)
