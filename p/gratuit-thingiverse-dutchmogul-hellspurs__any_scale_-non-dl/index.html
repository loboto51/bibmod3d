This is a bit of a preview of a much larger project we have in the works here at Ill Gotten Games, and I figured I'd share a couple of these pieces as I'm working through the early phases. To give some variety, you should open these up in 3d Builder (or a similar program) and re-size, mirror, stretch, and group to your heart's content. Both pieces print without support. The miniature shown is an unreleased model (also part of the upcoming project). You can keep track of the pieces in this series [in this growing collection](https://www.thingiverse.com/dutchmogul/collections/kingdoms-of-hell).

For those of you supporting us (or thinking about supporting us) on [Patreon](https://www.patreon.com/illgottengames), our patrons get special access to our store releases as they come out (not to mention license options to sell printed versions of our designs).

Enjoy!
