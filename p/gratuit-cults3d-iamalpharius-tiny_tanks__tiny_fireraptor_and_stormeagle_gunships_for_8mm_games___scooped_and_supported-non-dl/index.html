OK, just a scooped, supported and magnet ready version of Wolfkeeper's GRIMDARK MINUTE FLYING ARMED TRANSPORT and nitramthx's burning little dino for galactic crusaders.  They are great little free files and will work well at 8mm.  I sized them to have  roughly a 70mm wingspan, but size however you like, just be cognicent that resizing circles can make ovals.

I removed the lower floor of the model, so the magent insert is on the roof of the model and can accept magnets up to 10mm - so pick your poison.

I also did some editing to make sure there were no resin captures.  

These models are not hollowed in the traditional sense - no resin can be captured and all internals can be exposed to UV light for curing.  Some idiot will not look at the files and mis-understand that and will invariably do an "aktuallly blah blah blah" - it always seems to happen....
