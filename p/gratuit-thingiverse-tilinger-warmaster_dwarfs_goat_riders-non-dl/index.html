10mm dwarf goat riders for Warmaster. 2 command and 4 regular strips.

Yes, I know that there is no goat cavalry in the dwarf army list. I haven't even seen any in the Warhammer lore until now. But GreenBeanie asked whether I could make a cuple of iron hills dwarves. I am not a fan of how the movies depict dwarves though. In my mind they look something like this. Incidentally they look like 80s-90s Warhammer dwarfs.
