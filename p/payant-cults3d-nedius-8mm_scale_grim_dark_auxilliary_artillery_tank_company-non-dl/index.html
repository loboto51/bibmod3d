Rumbling forward on heavy tracks, the tanks of the Imperial Army are capable of decimating enemy forces with overwhelming firepower. The versatile main battle tank hull is capable of being adapted to feature a wide range of alternate patterns - including devastating artillery.

This set of 79 STLS, and features the heavy 'Bombardier' tank, the long range 'Basil' tank, and the medium 'Euryale' tank, each with variants with and without bulldozer blade, and variants with holes onto which plastic open hatches from comparable plastic tank kits are compatible. There are also additional hull variants, of each tank, including an Imperial Army Bombardier tank, and hulls with extra armour, plus some kit form Bombardiers. Also included are a range of an optional details, including rear-mounted mounted dozer blade (enabling the artillery to 'dig in' prior to firing), rear mounted trailers, support struts, and shells that can be used to add features to any bases you wish to mount the tanks on.

This set has been made to be compatible with 8mm scale main line battle tanks of the Imperial Army in a recently released 8mm grim-dark future wargame.

Please see my other items for a free sample of this set, and a range of other 8mm scale and other wargames and hobby models. I am an enthusiastic amateur designer, self and video-tutorial taught, so am always open to constructive feedback.
