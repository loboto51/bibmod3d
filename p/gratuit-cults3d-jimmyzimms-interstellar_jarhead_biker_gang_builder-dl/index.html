[fresh upload from my Thingiverse profile due to Grandma Wendy]

Provides a version of Moonjam's excellent Bikes and related things that have been sliced into major parts that are enabled to be quickly swapped and edited for customization by changing visibility of components between different options allowing any combination of biker/attack biker, weapons, and rider/gunner armour marks.

Also contains a custom built 40k style bike variant for first born marines plus the new primaris outrider bike.

This is intended for designers and modelers to product their own faction customized vehicles from in a standardized and high quality form.
Contains a zip with instructions on how to use this utility (MS word)

Leverages work from DangItBlobby, KillMeForPrizes, TheZomby, Moonjam, and of course, myself.

New part contributions are welcome!
