New Iron WIthin(tm) dietary supplement, containing over 5000% your daily iron intake! Iron Within, Iron Without!

Three Poses, choice of helmed or unhelmed.
Pose 1 has an option of a rock or a Dread for him to stand on.

Considering this model is bigger than the usual 8mm infantry, He comes on a set of standoffs - the model can be painted, then cut off the standoffs to be glued to a base.

Thanks to Moonjam for the original Galactic Crusaders.
Thanks to Mildseven for the Forgebreaker, and the Walking Tomb!
The bare head I used as a base for this is from MrMcAngry.
