Description :

~~~
This is a generic upper arm piece compatible with my RV-A series of guns. It's based on a model I found floating around on the internet - unfortunately I can't find the creator, but kudos to whoever made it - with a few modifications, so here it is for free for convenience's sake.

There are five variants, choose whichever fits your purposes:

RV Arm is the original model as-is.
RV Arm Elbow Magnet has a magnet well for a 5x1mm disc magnet cut into the lower end of the arm.
RV Arm Shoulder Magnet cuts the ball joint for the shoulder in half and adds a half-socket for a 5mm disc magnet to the top surface.
RV Arm Double Magnet has both the elbow magnet well and the shoulder half-socket.
Finally, the RV Arm Plug has a shoulder half-socket and a slightly-less-than-5mm diameter plug built into the elbow surface that'll fit into the magnet well of my RV-A series of guns.
~~~
