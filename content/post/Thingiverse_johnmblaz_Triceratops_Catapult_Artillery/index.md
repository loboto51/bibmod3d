~~~
This is a remix of the attached Triceratops model mixed with the Ewok Catapult (https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:4773367). I modeled the ammo bags and ropes for the catapult, as well as the leather strapping and harnesses, and enhanced the detail of everything in Zbrush. The triceratops' pose was adjusted slightly to get the catapult to not bang into his frill.

I sized this for a 40x80mm base for Hordes of the Things/ Fantasy Rules! (yes the ! is part of the name). I left the harness option in for people who want to risk printing it at it's current size, or who may want to embiggen it for display or to make a huge 28mm scale model. This one is sized roughly for my 10mm mass battle lizardman army.
