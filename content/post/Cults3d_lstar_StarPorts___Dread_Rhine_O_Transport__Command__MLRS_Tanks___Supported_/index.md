Description :

~~~
A humble reworking of JimmyZimms' excellent reworking of Woddish's excellent design, optimised for easy printing.

Command seems to think that deploying the troops without their transports will help them lose weight... Let's fix that!

- Supported and tweaked for easy printing
- Includes Transport, Command, and MLRS tanks with optional prongs and push bars, with a choice of boring closed hatches, or slots for modular hatches
- Includes Lychee Scenes for anyone wanting to tweak supports/rafts
- A Video Tutorial with advice for adding your own faction or squad logos to the split top hatches of these vehicles.
