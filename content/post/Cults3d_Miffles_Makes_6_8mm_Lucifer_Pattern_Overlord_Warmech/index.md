~~~
Based around the internal structure of Stroganoff's excellent 4th Planet Battleduke 

https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/various/4th-planet-battleduke-pre-olympian-god-stroganoff

All armor plating and pattern specific components of this model are modeled from scratch in XSI Softimage.
Intended to be used in small scale gaming so all tolerances are set for that scale, final model should stand roughly 5.5 inches tall. If you want to use it for the more popular scale of grimdark gaming you can scale it up by 363.5% but I cant guarantee any of the tolerances will work.

For extra weapons and accessories go here:

https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/6-8mm-extra-accessories-lucifer-pattern-overlord-goliath-warmech

Some armor plates include BLNK and V01/02 versions, these are variant pieces that have slight cosmetic differences to help customize your prints.

Includes ready made ports for magnetizing arm and shoulder weapon options, allowing you to swap weapon loadouts as you wish. Accepts 2x standard 3mm×8mm disc magnet in the body and a 6×32 machine nut in each weapon, both are available at most hardware stores 

Should not be printed to replace pre-existing miniatures options, please support the original manufacturer
