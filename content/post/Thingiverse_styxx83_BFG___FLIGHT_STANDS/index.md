Hey Guys,

here are my flight stands for BFG. 
I like it when the flight bases are adapted to the size of the ships they are carrying. 
While this may not be in line with the rules, it shouldn't play too much of a role.
Escort stand (25mm diam.) have only one deepening for a mini dice to represent the initiative.
(LD value of the ship = 6 + dice value)
light cruiser stands (30mm diam.) have two slots. (1 dice for structure points, second one for LD)
Battle-/Heavy-/Cruisers (35mm diam.)
(2 dices for SP, 1 for LD)
Grand Cruisers (40mm diam.)
(2 dices for SP, 1 for LD)
Battleships (60mm diam.)
(2 dices for SP, 1 for LD)
Ships like Planet Killer will get a customized one later.

I hope you like my creation. 
Have fun.
~~~
