Description :

~~~
Heavy Infantry for teleporting into your foes, Boarding their ships, and killing them dead, this time with twice as many claws!

Three armour types, Cata, Tart and Indom Patterns.
Tart and Indom pattern stuff has been mostly remodelled from previous models to improve the geometry and give them a bit more detail.

10 Poses.

Melee Weapon options for Dual Claws of both Claw A (Talons) and ClawB (Wolverine-Style)

Ordnance options include a grenade launcher and missile launchers.

Check out the "_standoffs" version. The models' feet are offset from the plug, allowing you to use the plug as a stand when painting, and then slice or snip off the small cylinders to allow you to glue the feet directly onto the base, for a seamless finish.

Thanks to Moonjammy, Jimmyzimms and KMFP who made the original Terminuses
