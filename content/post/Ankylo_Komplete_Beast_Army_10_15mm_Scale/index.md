Description :

~~~
This set contains the stls for a complete "Beast" army useable for warmaster, minihammer, etc.





Here is a list of single sets:





The Base Set comes with 12 strips (6 miniatures per strip) representing two different kind of Beast Units (small beasts with bow and small+medium beasts mixed).





All strips in two versions: unsupported + pre-supported.





Additionally you get the stls for the single miniatures (also unsupported + pre-supported) plus three different chariots with crew.





The chariots and the crew are separate stls so you can paint the crew before gluing it to the chariot.





The complete base package consists of over 40 stls (not counting the supported versions).





-


Next one 6 strips of hard hitting Big Beasts, three Dragon Hybrids and 6 strips of Centaurs.  





The single miniatures for the Big Beasts and the Centaurs are also included.





All stls unsupported + pre-supported.





-


3 strips of Monstrous Beasts (inculding 9 singles), 12 warhounds, one Gigantic Beast, four Characters to lead the army to battle and a massive Stone Idol.





All stls in unsupported + pre-supported versions.





- Three different abominations (unsupported and pre-supported) + two casualty markers.





Please take a look at the pictures.


If you have further questions, pls contact us.





Thank you for your support and kind regards


Ankylo Miniatures
