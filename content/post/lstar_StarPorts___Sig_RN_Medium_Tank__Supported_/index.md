Description :

~~~
A humble reworking of JimmyZimms' excellent reworking of Woddish's excellent design, optimised for easy printing.

- Supported and tweaked for easy printing
- Includes command options, and a remixable hatch
- 4 turret options with holes for 3mm magnets for easy gun swapping
- Pack includes a masterfully designed 3x2.5mm cylinder to align turrets for people who don't like magnets
- Sponsons are cut and keyed for easy assembly, and are compatible with my other easy print packs
- Includes Lychee Scenes for anyone wanting to tweak supports/rafts
- Includes optional "scooped" hulls to save a few pence of resin
