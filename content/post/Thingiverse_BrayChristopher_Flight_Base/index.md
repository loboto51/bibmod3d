I'm not a fan of the traditional plastic flight stands for flying miniatures. They tend to be brittle and weak in my experience.

This was designed in TinkerCAD Codeblocks so that is can be easily adjusted to fit the size you want.

https://www.tinkercad.com/codeblocks/a8A3BhQCXXA

The idea to glue the 3D printed dome base onto a washer for weight, and glue a nail into the hole in the dome. 

I am using commonly available fender washer sizes and 4G nails. There is a magnet in to bottom of the space ship.


The space ship is by Tezca.

https://www.thingiverse.com/tezca/designs

https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3551239
~~~
