Main battle tank for the tiny humans army. 
Basic, sniping and close range variants included.

Prints well at 0.02, but some parts may break (first time with clear resin so it is probably my fault, as after curing they hold on pretty well). The long barrelled turret may require some reinforcement (or not), I just drilled it and run a pin through it.

At some point in the future I may incude versions with characters on top (I modified the turret hatch to be easy to print once opened, but never found the time to actually do them).
