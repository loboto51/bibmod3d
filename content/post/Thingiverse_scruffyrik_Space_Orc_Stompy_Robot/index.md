~~~
Inspired by the fantastic Mark Gibbons "Ork Stompa Gargant" drawing.

They were drawn by me in Fusion 360 and printed on my Anycubic photon at 0.05mm layer height.  There are 3 poses with 3 different guns.

They are approximately Epic/6mm scaled and are around 30mm tall.  They fit on a 25mm base just about.  

Designed for resin printing I doubt it will work in FDM but I've never tried!

Update:
Added the component parts but they parts (Particularly the arms) have overlaps so need to be assembled in something like meshmixer prior to printing.  They will not fit together if printed separately.  If I have time I will sort this...
~~~
