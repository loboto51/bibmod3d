~~~
An alternative shield and arm for a Cerastus Knight Lancer, made for the AT2018 Cerastus Knight.

Along with the new shield, the arm comes in 3 pieces; upper arm, forearm connector and hand. This allows the forearm to be angled as required and the hand rotated to any angle, facilitating it to be used as a left or right hand, as desired.
Shield on its own is also compatible with the original Cerastus Knight Lancer arms.

Files included: STL, pre-supported STL and full Lychee scene of all parts (in the ZIP file) - should you want/need to tweak the supports for your particular printer.

This piece is made to complement my (Free) Cerastus Knight Trident Lance, available here:
https://cults3d.com/:941267


Note:
This is for the 8mm, AT2018, Cerastus knights
As with the Cerastus Trident Lance, the upper arm comes with a moulded pin so that it can be swapped in and out (once the corresponding hole has been drilled in the original shoulder piece). Just cut this off after printing if you do not intend to swap weapons on your Cerastus Knight.
