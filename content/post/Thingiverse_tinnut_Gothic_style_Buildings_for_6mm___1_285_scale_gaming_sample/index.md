~~~
This is a sample of a collection of many gothic sci-fi buildings, for 6mm / 1:285 gaming - primarily Battletech and Epic 40k.  I'm selling the full set on [cults3d here](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/gothic-style-buildings-for-6mm-1-285-scale-gaming) to help justify to my significant other the time I spend working on these :)

The buildings are combinations of tower blocks that equate to level 1, 2 and 3 for when used within Battletech. There are two types of roof, flat and peeked as well as some other embellishments which means all up there are 290 different buildings in the cults3d set, although they're all different combinations of tower blocks.

For Battletech, they are designed to fit entirely within the "standard" Battletech map hexes of 33mm flat edge to flat edge as is used in the current boxed sets. The flat roof versions are also designed to allow Mech models to be placed on them.

The models are designed to be mostly supportless on an FDM printer and have printed fine on my Ender 3.

Edit:: I've repaired the files.
~~~
