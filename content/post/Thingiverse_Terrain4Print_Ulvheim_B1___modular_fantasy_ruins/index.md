I revisit the city of Ulvheim. This time we leave to craftsmen quarters and come to the powerfull nobles and rich merchants. Ulvheim B is a modular set of pieces that can be used to make relative intact as well as ruin buildings. You can print as many as you like and make something really gigantic.

I have tried to make a manual in with the images. Please comment if you have opinions on this.

The flat pieces use the shorter peg. Walls use the regular peg. For best stability, glue the tapered end of the peg into the hole from the bottom of each piece.
