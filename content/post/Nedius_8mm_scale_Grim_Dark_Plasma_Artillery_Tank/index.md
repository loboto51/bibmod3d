Rumbling forward on heavy tracks, the tanks of the Imperial Army are capable of decimating enemy forces with overwhelming firepower. The versatile main battle tank hull is capable of being adapted to feature a wide range of alternate patterns - including devastating artillery.

This is a single hull sample of a set of artillery tank hulls  - The full set contains a Bombardier, Medusan, and Basilic variant hulls. 

This sample hull has been made to be compatible with 8mm scale main line battle tanks of the Imperial Army in a recently released 8mm grim-dark future wargame.
