This is my proxy design for an epic scale space bug Dactylis.  I've based it off the fantastic design of a scratch built one in 40k scale that is on the internet - I'm afraid that mine doesn't do it justice but it was a lot of fun making it.  Because of this it doesn't look anything like the original model that's well out of print.

To design it, I built the basic body shape in Tinkercad with simple shapes and then transferred that over to Blender and sculpted it heavily from there.
