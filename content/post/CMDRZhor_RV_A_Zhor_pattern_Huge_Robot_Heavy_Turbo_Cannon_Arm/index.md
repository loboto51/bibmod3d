This is an enormous heavy energy weapon arm for huge battle robots. The elbow joint has a well for a 5mm magnet in it, along with interface ports to make it compatible with the WB series of slightly-bigger-than-huge battle robots.

Edit 4/21/22: The original Heavy Turbo Cannon was kind of a chonky lad. I made an upgraded 'Slim' version, essentially scaling it down by 15%, then extending the barrel and rejiggering the mounts to fit it again - giving it a lighter, sleeker form factor, while still remaining a giant fuckoff cannon. I also smoothed out some of the geometry to give the main chassis a more rounded look. 

I also went and smoothed out the geometry on the original one while I was at it - both are included in this download, the original being the 'Big Smoothed' version, while the newer lighter model is the 'Slim' version. Enjoy!

Fixed a geometry error with the trim on the model, uploaded as the 'Fix' versions. Sorry for the inconvenience.
