Description :

~~~
This is a remix of the impressive STROGANOFF original Epic Scale  Landing Craft found at https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/epic-scale-landing-craft. As I wanted to print it on FDM I modified it to have the less non-fdm printable detail removed (turrets, landing gear) and those details later printed in a resin printer for accuracy. 
There is some work in the lateral clamps as I have added a 4mm magnet slot and modified the width to fit new rhinos/big grimdark containers.

* 2024-02-10 Update: Add missing lower rear reactor piece and update renders

I have explicitly asked STROGANOFF for modifying and sharing this asset. Be respectful with your fellow artists :)
~~~
