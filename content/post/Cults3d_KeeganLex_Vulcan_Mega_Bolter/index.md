Description :

~~~
I personally love Warhound Titans and have a full size one printed and painted, though I was never a fan of how the Vulcan Mega Bolters look like a brick with a magazine which would run empty after 5 seconds of continuous fire.  This is my attempt to make something more visually striking and with a magazine more suitable for such an immense weapon.  The arm mount is sized to other arm mounts which can be found on Cults for free.  Just to mix things up a bit, I created a version of the Mega Bolter with twin short barrels and long barrels.  The full pack comes with two complete Bolters with short/long barrel along with separate parts for those who want to print separate objects.  As always, hope you all enjoy and don't hesitate to contact me with any concerns you may have.

The photographed Bolter is a test print using Nylon 12.
~~~
