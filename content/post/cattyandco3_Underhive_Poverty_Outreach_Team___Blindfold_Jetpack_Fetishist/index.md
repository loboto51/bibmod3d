~~~
The Underhive Poverty Outreach Team are made up of the members of the Noble Houses. They are equipped with special suits of armor, which they use to stock the many food banks found in the Underhive.

Christine Held's role in the team, as a Blindfold Jetpack Fetishist, is to provide aerial surveillance of working class hive-domes and identify hungry people - to whom she lowers groceries using her webgun. To suit other urgent needs during the cost of living crisis she can also dispense clean hypodermic needles from her needlegun.
