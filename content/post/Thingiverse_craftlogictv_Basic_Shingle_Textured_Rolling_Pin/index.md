This is the shingle texture roller that I demonstrated on the Craft Logic YouTube channel. This is a technique I am experimenting with for making good looking shingles much more quickly than cutting and gluing individual pieces. This is still a work in progress. I have printed a very similar roller successfully but have since made changes and this version has not be printed. The first version was printed on my Ender 3 without any issues and without supports. The changes I made are fairly minimal so I expect this will print just fine as well. I have linked to the demonstration video on my channel, but, to be honest, it isn't the best example of what the roller can do. I have also included a picture of another test piece that has much better results in my opinion. I will keep experimenting, but I'd also love to get feedback on the roller if you decide to print and use it! Let me know what you think!
