Description :

~~~
This is thrown together mostly built on the hull of MGL89DK's Solaris Cruiser (https://www.thingiverse.com/MGL89DK/about), with a bit from Printalotapatomus (https://www.thingiverse.com/printalotapotamus/about), with the fairings, wings and blade added by myself. Special thanks to François Gand for his online images of a 3d model he sculpted on Art Station which inspired me (https://f_gand.artstation.com/projects/4b66zW)

The dorsal/ventral blades slip into a slot at the back of the hull assembly, the fairings line up roughly with the length of the hull's nose, and the wings are about the same size as the 30˚ slopes on which they are meant to lie. 

NOTE: This prints at around 120mm, which is in scale with my own fleets, so scale it to your liking
~~~
