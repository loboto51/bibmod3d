With the mysterious Dark Powers making their once-hidden presence more known, new, sinister mech designs have begun to appear on the battlefields of the Junctionworld inter-reality gate networks.

Notable among these new nightmares is a tailed, tentacled monstrosity labeled by intel operatives at the Mercenary Guild as Project Abby.

These warped, spiked, and twisted armors are malevolent in form and purpose.  They have a heat cannon on one arm as their primary ranged weapon, while the melee component of their arsenal is composed of an energized whip weapon system.  Crackling with plasma, this cluster of mechanical tentacles can be used to impale, crush, or slice through enemy targets.  The Abby features a tail-like appendage that performs the same function, protecting the armored walker from the rear.

Project Abby mechs also seem to be nesting grounds for ScytheBots, verminous robot drones that seem to claim the dreaded hulks as mobile nests.  These hive-like bots provide a symbiotic layer of protection for the mech, cutting down infantry that venture too close.
