Description :

~~~
3 different 15mm Ork Tanks

These are purely a remix from the 3 modelers I have tagged in the remix section, so all credit to them ! (the are originally grot tanks)

I have ...

- Resized / rescaled some of the guns and detail (or removed / solidified it) to enable print at 15mm and be Ork tanks.
- Made them all into three specific parts for ease of print, Turret, Body and 2 sets of tracks (where they were not already or in different parts).
- Hollowed out and added drain holes for Resin 3D printing.
- Added a recessed hole in the body and turret to allow the use of a 10x2mm magnet so the turret can turn or be swapped.
- Scaled in size overall to 15mm

The models in parts are in the zip files.
~~~
