~~~
I hate clear plastic flight stands. They are brittle and they break. And the model ends up top heavy.

This flight stand incorporates a wide base, hefty fillet, and the stem is reinforced with a nail.

The idea is that you put a magnet into the bottom of the model stand stick to the head of the nail at the top of the stem.

Use the TinkerCAD link below to adjust the size to your exact needs.

https://www.tinkercad.com/codeblocks/evJFuP7PBiF
