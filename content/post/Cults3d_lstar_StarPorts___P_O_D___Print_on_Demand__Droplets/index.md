Description :

~~~
A humble reworking of Speacock and THEWRAETHTHU's excellent reworking of Failtruck's (?) excellent design, optimised and cleaned up for easy printing.

"_While P.O.D. Droplets were usually synonymous with lightning assaults, one of their most infamous uses during the Hera-C involved an over-eager commander ordering the launch of a wave long before his logistics ships had made it into orbit._

_When the P.O.D.s finally made planetfall some six months later, the confused defenders found nothing inside but dust and emaciated corpses..._" 

- Reassembled and repaired: No more voids!
- Hollowed for efficient printing: Including rafts and supports, small P.O.D.s use ~3.8ml of resin, large P.O.D.s use ~5.4ml of resin
- Includes supported and unsupported STLs and Lychee scenes for tweaking
