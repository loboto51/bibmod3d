The Elite Assault troops of the Bloody Boys, equipped with Spears, wrist mounted Grenade Launchers, and Jump Packs!

Ground poses
13 Poses

Air Poses
Troops in the air have a 1mm hole for insertion of a paperclip or 1mm nylon thread to act as a flight stand.
12 Poses

Jumping poses can be glued to terrain to give some extra dynamism!

Check out the "_standoffs" version. The models' feet are offset from the plug, allowing you to use the plug as a stand when painting, and then slice or snip off the small cylinders to allow you to glue the feet directly onto the base, for a seamless finish.
