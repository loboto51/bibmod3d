Description :

~~~
This is a collection of pre-constructed hulls of the Auxiliary Heavy Tanks, with and without Dozer Blades and trench rails. 

Each combination of main gun and hull gun is included, making it easier to print exactly the hull combination you want without them having to construct the kits. There are a total of 78 hull combinations, and the files for the 4 sponson types.  

The main set has been made to be complimentary with 8mm scale battle tanks of a recently released 8mm grim-dark future wargame.

Please see my other items for a free sample of this set, and a range of other 8mm scale and other wargames and hobby models. I am an enthusiastic amateur designer, self and video-tutorial taught, so am always open to constructive feedback.

Please consider liking the design and posting a make.
