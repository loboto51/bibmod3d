Der wer big oh-gerz on this world and da grots stole der bins fer der tank turrets. Who sez any ol rubbish makes good shooty tank turrets iz right.

Idea was using a trashcan turret similar to the early scorcher model. If you want me to make it smaller I can do so by making a reducer ring to the turret adapter on the tank bodies. Aim is to eventually enhance the variability of grot tanks and to offer more bits for future kit bashing. Turret can be used with or without the mantle section.

Turret taken from Trash can with removable lid by Woodmore would fit Grot Tank and Grot Walking Tank by AgentSmith99. Made some weapons available to fit from gaming tank cannon collection by bulldog2708. Alt turret hatch was 28mm tank hatch (open and closed) by Forpost_D6 and Ork Skull Glyph was from 40K Ork Symbols
by kombatunit.
