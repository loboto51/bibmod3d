Description :

~~~
Injured Crusaders for sprinkling across your bases for titans, or as fallen enemies on your own bases!

10 poses of dead or dying galactic crusaders.
Options for all five armour variants, Segmented, Breaching, Plate, Ball Bearing or Beaky.

Check out the "_standoffs" version. The models' feet are offset from the plug, allowing you to use the plug as a stand when painting, and then slice or snip off the small cylinders to allow you to glue the feet directly onto the base, for a seamless finish.

Thanks to Moonjammy, for the base models, and MrMcAngry for the base bare head.
