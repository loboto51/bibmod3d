Description :

~~~
The All-Purpose Carrier lives up to its name as a rugged and dependable platform that has been adapted to almost every role on & off the battlefield.

This all-in-one package of M'khand-pattern All-Purpose Carrier parts allows you to build your ideal APC-based vehicle to your exact needs. Contains all previously released APC-chassis related files***, both free and paid, adapted to 1:300 scale for mass-scale wargames. 

Turrets and middles have 2.5x1.25mm holes for magnets. Compatible with all epic scale M'khand-pattern APC & Lemoine Russel turrets.

To use meshmixer builder, open with meshmixer (a free program from autodesk) and ctrl-click in the object browser the items you wish to have as one piece. Then go to File and Export. Your merged piece is ready for repair and slicing.

Alternatively, individual part stls can be assembled in 3Dbuilder or another program of your choice where they should snap together.

***Non-IFV turrets, Christie suspension sides and artillery cannons for unarmoured hulls not included***

2022-12-30: Added track guards to the builder.

2022-12-30: Added new options for sponson and hull-mounted weapons.

2024-01-23: Added missing armoured artillery mounts and cannons.

2024-08-20: Renamed the parts to be much easier to read. Added new turret parts, new SPG, SPM, and scout tank middles, and wheeled sides (wheels themselves should be printed separately)

2024-09-26: Added hatches for side weapon arrays
