Tiny tanks! The perfect thing to give a sense of scale to your tiny titans, tiny aircraft, or tiny anything! Add them as base decoration, add them as terrain decoration, create tiny war dioramas, do whatever you want with them! Anything is made better with the addition of tiny tanks.

We've long since jumped the shark when it comes to tiny biggest tank designs. But just in case you're one of those tiny generals who is sure that more gunz is even better here's the overcompensation tank.

Tiny tanks are intended for resin printers.

You should print 1 chassis, 4 point defense turrets, and 2 main turrets (one mirrored) for each tank you want.

A heavily reworked version of the awesome TINY BIGGEST TANK MBT by Woddish with custom glacis and turrets crafted to visually resemble the design cues of the Tiny Bigger Tank MBT to bridge the two designs. The main turret is fully interchangeable with that tank (for additional modeling joy). The smaller turrets will fully work with my TINY BIGGEST TANK REDUX as well. This was inspired by the brilliant 40k kitbash as well as the classic 2nd Edition epic figure.

Blender source included!

Update 20221222: TheZomby has graciously provided not one but two new alternate main turrets for our use! Big ups!

Update 2023.04.03: Added an additional hatch on the upper deck of the chassis. The area looked a little empty of details in real life.
