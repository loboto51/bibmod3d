~~~
Rumbling forward on heavy tracks, the tanks of the Imperial Army are capable of decimating enemy forces with overwhelming firepower. The versatile main battle tank is capable of being adapted to house a great range of weaponry, ensuring the right tool is available to tackle any foe. Whether busting bunkers, demolishing buildings, bathing entrenched foes in flames or caustic chemicals, or melting through armour plate, there is a turret for every occasion.

This is a sample turret from my full set of tank turrets featuring 15 weapon options, plus two with additional coaxial cannons, making a total of 17 weapon load outs. Each of those comes with optional designs, making this set of over 60 STLs!

Find the full set here: https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/8mm-scale-grim-dark-tank-turrets-of-russ-nedius

This turret has been made to be compatible with 8mm scale main line battle tanks of the Imperial Army in a recently released 8mm grim-dark future wargame. It features a magnet hole, and a peg compatible with existing plastic model hulls. 

I've provided this free sample to support those hobbyists who want an turret option, and are happy with just that one option! However, the full set is priced very cheaply! I deeply appreciate all those who support and encourage my work by buying any of my paid designs.

Please see my other items for the full version of this set, and a range of other 8mm scale and other wargames and hobby models. I am an enthusiastic amateur designer, self and video-tutorial taught, so am always open to constructive feedback.
