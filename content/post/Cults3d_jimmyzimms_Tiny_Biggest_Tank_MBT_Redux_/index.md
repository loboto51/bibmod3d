A quick enhancement to Woddish's beautiful Tiny Biggest Tank MBT to allow better modeling usability. I've extracted the and plinthed all the secondary weapon turrets for separate printing, painting, and modeling purposes. I've also repositioned the sponsons on the more traditionally modeled middle section of the tank instead of rear.

Edit 20230310: Based on a request I've produced an alternate hull that allows the sponsons to be printed separately. Some amount of filing and fitting may be required based on your printer tolerances.
