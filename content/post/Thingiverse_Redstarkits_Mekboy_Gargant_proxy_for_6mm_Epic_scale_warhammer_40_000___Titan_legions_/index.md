The smallest of the 4 Gargant types the mekboy fits on the standard gw 40mm base or old monster base.

I did have some trouble printing this model on my ender 3. i found some of the bits too small and cleanup to be a pain. So i have included simplified versions of the parts that gave me the most trouble. mainly the large arm, (with or without pistons) the buzz saw (wide for PLA printers) and the grabber claw 2 or 3 fingers.
The arms can be flipped so you can swap the chain saw and guns as you like. i have made the arms quit long compaired to the origional as i never liked the stubby little arms of the metal model. 
Also you can flip the big weapon mount if you want the big arm on the right side, or print one each shoulder and have a 4 armed gargant
