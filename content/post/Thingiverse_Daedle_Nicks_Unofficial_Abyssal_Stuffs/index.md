THESE ARE ENTIRELY UNOFFICIAL AND THIRD PARTY. NOTHING TO DO WITH MANTIC GAMES.

These are the custom parts I sculpted and got printed for my Kings of War Abyssals army. They are completely unofficial and are not produced, licensed or connected in any way to Mantic.

There are 3 files;
Arug - Head: A custom head for my Archfiend, Arug Or'Gal
Arug - Mace: A custom mace and right hand for my Archfiend model
Arug - Screaming Skull: This is a 3D version of my army logo that I used for objective markers

The head and mace will require conversion work for the archfiend model - you will need to trim away excess from both the custom part and the original and fill with greenstuff. They don't simply slot into place, you will need to do some chopping and converting.

For printing, I ordered through 3DHubs.com with a resin printer at max resolution.
