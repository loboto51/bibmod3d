Description :

~~~
An expanded set for Tactical Galactic Crusaders, with Gloomy Flavour in multiple armour marks, including Segmented, Boarding, Plate, New Style Ball Bearing and New Style Beaky.
Now with robes for your monastic, knightly or fashion conscious space lads.

Each pose has an option of a tabard, robe, or a hooded robe.

Weapons available are rifles with two types of bayonet, plasma, flamethrower, magma gun, heat ray, and gravitic rifles. Pretty much any option you could want in a rifle carrying future guy!

Tactical Galactic Crusaders in a variety of poses, including Sergeants, Radio, Grenade-throwing, Scanner, and Back banner as add-ons for some additional variation.

Check out the "_standoffs" version. The models' feet are offset from the plug, allowing you to use the plug as a stand when painting, and then slice or snip off the small cylinders to allow you to glue the feet directly onto the base, for a seamless finish.

Thanks to Moonjammy, DangitBlobby and JimmyZimms for the base models, the Ball Bearing Armour and Beaky Armour, on which this is based.
A BIG thanks to LSTAR, who helped me with the export script for these guys. I wouldn't be able to put out the variety I have without it!

If you have any requests or would like to comission a remix, please let me know!

If you like what I'm doing, please donate to my ko-fi!
www.ko-fi.com/daffmakes
