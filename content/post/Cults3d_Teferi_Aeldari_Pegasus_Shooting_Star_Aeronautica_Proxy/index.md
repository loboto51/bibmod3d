This is a 3D Model I created to introduce a heavy Landing Vehicle for my Aeronautica Games - all credit for the genius LOOK and DESIGN goes to archicks, whose GLORIOUS scratchbuild of the original 40k scale Pegasus you can find here: http://eldar.arhicks.co.uk/miniatures/eldar/craftworld/pegasus_1.php

I appended the "shooting star" name, because that is what the HALO insertion Vehicle for the Swooping Hawks is actually called, a large transport skimming into athmosphere, looking like a shooting star, dipping just low enough to safely insert the Swooping Hawks into a planets Airspace - and I think if there is as of yet not-modelled but in the background described vehicles, it aidens nobody to just introduce more and more vehicles and names which will be harder and harder to keep track off :)
~~~
