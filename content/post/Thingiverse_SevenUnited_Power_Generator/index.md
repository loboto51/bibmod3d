A generator for wargaming. Sector Mechanicus compatible.

The small pipe connections fit the Sector Mechanicus pipelines and have 26mm diameter.

The connections of the Side_A and Side_B parts are at 61mm height which is the same than the height of the Ferratonic Furnace connection. The diameters of these are 26mm and 40mm.

-Added base_V2: allows you to put Side_B parts at both sides
