Description :

~~~
This is a remix of the [Chaos Hell Blade proxy CoolosaurusRex designed at 40K scale](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/chaos-flying-bident), yet reduced in size for use in Aeronautica Imperialis.  All credit for the design goes to him.  

The only modifications I made were the following:  reduction in scale to be proportional in scale with other A.I. models such as the Thunderbolt, merging the parts to single piece, reduction of the number of triangles (given the scale and challenges making the model manifold as a combined piece), and the addition of a standard A.I. peg hole on the ventral surface for a standard ball-type flight stand support.There are two models: One variant is equipped with quad Autocannons, and the other has twin lascannons.
