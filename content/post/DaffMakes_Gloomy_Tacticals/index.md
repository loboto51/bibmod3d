Description :

~~~
An expanded set for Tactical Galactic Crusaders, with Gloomy Flavour in multiple armour marks, including Segmented, Boarding, Plate, and New Style Beaky.

Each pose has an option of a tabard, robe, or a hooded robe.

Weapons available are rifles with two types of bayonet, plasma, flamethrower, magma gun, heat ray, and gravitic rifles. Pretty much any option you could want in a rifle carrying future guy!

Tactical Galactic Crusaders in a variety of poses, including Sergeants, Radio, Grenade-throwing, Scanner, and Back banner as add-ons for some additional variation.

Check out the "_standoffs" version. The models' feet are offset from the plug, allowing you to use the plug as a stand when painting, and then slice or snip off the small cylinders to allow you to glue the feet directly onto the base, for a seamless finish.
