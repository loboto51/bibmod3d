Description :

~~~
Inspired by the excellent work of Mortalitis, with their Warhound weapon rack (found here: https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/adeptus-titanicus-legions-imperialis-warhound-weapon-rack)

This rack works with Reaver or Warlord weapons. Intended to go into Really Useful Boxes fitted with rubberized/ferrous magnetic sheet. Or metal biscuit tins or whatever magnetic thing you're using.

Base magnets are 6x2mm per Mortalitis' design. However, there are 3x holes in my design; I felt that the bigger weapons needed more grip, though you can ignore the central magnet if you like.

The weapon mounting magnets are 5x1mm.
This is because the 'standard' magnet size for 8mm scale Reaver and Warlord titans is is 5x1mm (the plastic has holes in these sizes!). And also I have a lot of that size, so thats what I used.
~~~
