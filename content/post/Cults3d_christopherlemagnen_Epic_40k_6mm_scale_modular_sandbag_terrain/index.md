Decided to make some terrain for use with epic 40k, and here's the second part! This is a set of modular sandbag walls in two different heights that are designed to fit together on their own base to make them easier to move and keep together. The base is also the height of a standard epic infantry base, so they should look good with your army. If these prove popular I'll expand the range with bunkers, gun emplacements, and other kinds of terrain. I've also made a standalone sandbags set if you want them to easily conform to a normal base, https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/epic-40k-6mm-scale-standalone-sandbag-terrain
