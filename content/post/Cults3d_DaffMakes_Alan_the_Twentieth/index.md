Description :

~~~
is it actually Alan, or maybe his brother, Owen? Hard to tell.

Four Poses, choice of helmed or unhelmed.

Considering this model is bigger than the usual 8mm infantry, He comes on a set of standoffs - the model can be painted, then cut off the standoffs to be glued to a base.
Pose1 also comes with a rubble base to stick him down onto.

Most of this was sculpted from scratch, but nevertheless, thanks to Moonjam for the original Galactic Crusaders.
The bare head I used as a base for this is from MrMcAngry.

If you have any requests for additional variants, or would like to comission a remix, please let me know!
