Do you find an endless rain of bullets overwhelming your plastic men?  Do they need to carry a list of all their favorite vacation spots with them on the battlefield?  Do you hate having generic accessories on those tiny plastic arms?  If so, this may be the kit for you.

Each shield has a circular cutout in the back, in which to put the grip in.  It's circular so you can have them hold the shield at any angle.  These have not been test printed yet, so if you're worried, repair them in 3d builder first.  These probably aren't an exact match for the original but should work well enough for upgrading old and new marines.  Would look great on bladeguard vets!  The hand is from LoggyKs linked thing.  The skull is the legendary Brexit skull.
