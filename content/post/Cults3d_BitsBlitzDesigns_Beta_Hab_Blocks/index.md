~~~
The Beta Hab Blocks are designed for 6mm / 8mm gaming, think of games such as Battletech, Adeptus Titanicus, or Epic.

The kit consists of five different buildings. It comes in both single piece versions and with removable roof hatches (for those who need drainage holes when printing).

The dimensions:
-Building 1: 110mm wide x 55mm deep x 113mm tall
-Building 2: 110mm wide x 55mm deep x 81mm tall
-Building 3: 110mm wide x 55mm deep x 65mm tall
-Building 4: 110mm wide x 55mm deep x 129mm tall
-Building 5: 110mm wide x 55mm deep x 97mm tall
