Description :

~~~
When your political officer needs a tank to drive him closer to the enemy so he can hit them with his sword.

A quick-and-dirty mashup of the turret from here:
https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/russ-turret-with-commander-hatch
And the commissars from here:
https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:4723793

Scaled for 6mm Epic.

Could be printed by itself to attach to an existing vehicle, or you could splice it onto an existing model and print them together. As an example, I've included a ready-spliced turret from here:
https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:4351764
I've scaled the hatch and officer up 125% to fit on the turret as Woddish's designs are scaled for AT/8mm rather than traditional Epic 6mm.
~~~
