Description :

~~~
This is a 6mm residential building for use with 6mm - 1/285 war games, such as Battletech and Epic 40k.  It's designed to fix neatly within Battletech standard 33mm hexes like those found on the more recent maps at least.

I've tried to reduce the requirement for supports and on my print I can turn them off altogether on my Ender 3.

This is actually a sample from a much larger collection over here: https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/residential-buildings-for-6mm-1-285-scale-gaming.  That collection currently includes over 160 buildings in four different styles and I'll continue to expand it in the future. 

 A free sample of the other style can be found here: https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:4688152.

I sell the complete collection to help justify the amount of time I spend doing this, but I think I have enough free models to game with.

Edit May 2021:  I've uploaded a version of this model that is more resin friendly as I've removed all the internal geometry.
~~~
