Description :

~~~
Heavy Infantry for teleporting into your foes, Boarding their ships, and killing them dead!

I came up with some variants to represent wartime production of the Indom Pattern, as it was used to mass-produce terminus armour when materials or technology to produce the other patterns became scarce.

Options are the same as my other Terminus packs, including Ranged Weapons, Shields, Heavy Weapons and Dual Claws. Check out the options in the animations in the images!

Note that I've omitted the ordnance variations in order to save space (this is quite a big pack!). If you want grenade launchers or missile launchers, grab the bits from my other models and kitbash them on!

Check out the "_standoffs" version. The models' feet are offset from the plug, allowing you to use the plug as a stand when painting, and then slice or snip off the small cylinders to allow you to glue the feet directly onto the base, for a seamless finish.

Thanks to Moonjammy, Jimmyzimms and KMFP who made the original Terminuses
A BIG thanks to LSTAR, who helped me with the export script for these guys. I wouldn't be able to put out the variety I have without it!

While uploading, i noticed some of the studs are way higher detail than they need to be! Apologies! Hopefully you should be able to exclusively download the files you need, but I hope they shouldn't be too bad!
