Description :

~~~
Available for deployment!

This tiny tank represents an older generation of imperial MBTs. Despite its seeming obsolescence many still soldier on. Perfect for games involving tiny robots or tanks in 8mm scale.

This set contains 2 hull weapon options along with numerous sponson gun variations. Uparmored engine compartment variant for siege tank is provided along with track guard option.

4 turret variants are provided:

- standard cannon
- 2 variants of a siege howitzer
- dual automatic cannon

Furthermore, this tank is compatible with other turrets made for similar pattern of mbt made before and hopefully after this one.

Turrets can be printed with a commander cupola opened (heroic tank officers will have to be sourced separately for the time being)

UPDATE 12/12/2024 - fixed a small issue with trackside geometry
~~~
