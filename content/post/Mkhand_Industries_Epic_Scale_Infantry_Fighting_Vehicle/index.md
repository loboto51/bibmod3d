Description :

~~~
A true-scale 6mm version of the M'khand Pattern Infantry Fighting Vehicle. Some details have been removed and others altered to make them more viable at this scale.

Perfect for playing the various editions of Epic and as base toppers for Adeptus Titanicus 2018.

2021-04-23: Added a ZIP with a hulls that have no details on the underside of the tracks and body, greatly reducing islands. Also added in hulls with tiny small arms arrays.

2021-11-24: Forgot to put in the updated turrets with holes to fit 2x1mm magnets. Removed the zip. Left the old files for those who don't want to worry about rivets on their tiny IFVs.

2022-01-17: Added new plainer turrets. Added the most current files combined as a 3mf file.

2022-07-03: Added turrets with new autocannons and single heavy machine guns. Removed the last of the old files.

2024-09-26: Added hulls with hatches covering their arrays
~~~
