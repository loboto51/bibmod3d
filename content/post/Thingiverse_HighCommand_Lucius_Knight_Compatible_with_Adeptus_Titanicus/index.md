Description :

~~~
I prefer the angular armour of the Lucius pattern titans rather than the rounded armour of the official GW miniatures, so I designed this Knight-size titan to be compatible with Adeptus Titanicus scale.

It is slightly larger than the GW Knight miniatures, so you could scale it down a bit if you prefer to be more exact. I like the chunky look. For use in Epic you would need to scale down even further - not sure the exact proportion (see 'Alternate Scale Prints' section below).

This is designed to be very easy to print. All parts except the legs can be printed without supports. I would be open to any ideas about how to redesign the legs to print without supports (I have considered making them multipart). I've had good success printing at both 0.06 and 0.1 resolution, with 10-20% infill.

There are three weapon options and two leg options. The banner between the legs is optional. I hope to design some more heads and some weapons for use as Cerastus.
~~~
