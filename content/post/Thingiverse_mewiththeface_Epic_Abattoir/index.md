~~~
My take on the Epic Necron War Engine. The canoptek machine holds a C'tan in eternal punishment, keeping its core ripped open and perpetually wounded, searching for sustenance. Ghastly claws extend down from its exposed torso, grasping at any organisms it can find to pull into its chest and absorb their essence. Punishment for the C'tan and living alike, the Abattoir serves the Silent King well. 

It should fit on 2 prints. Not a super easy model to print and not intended for any larger scale. I have no intentions of separating it further. 

Please enjoy!
