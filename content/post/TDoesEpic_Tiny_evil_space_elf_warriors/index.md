6mm cabal warriors for your tiny evil space elves to run amock and be absolute jerks to everybody. 
This set includes

6 regular warriors armed with thorn rifles
2 gunners armed with thorn cannons
1 leader with thorn pistol and blade
1 leader with thorn rifle 

I recommend mirroring half your print for more variety, it seems unlikely these nasty space elves would stick to any kind of handedness (except underhandedness) 

A supported version is included for every design.

Keep in mind these are quite slim and fragile figures, so be careful while removing supports! 

The detail on these should look good to about 15mm if scaled up, any larger (like 28mm) and they will start to look awfully bobblehead and disproportion. 

Hopefully more to come soon!

UPDATE 15/04/23:
I've added four "riders" which can be attached to vehicles like my skiffs, to give them some more style!
