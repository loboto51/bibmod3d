Description :

~~~
Truescale 6mm versions of M'khand Pattern Self Propelled Artillery carriers. Comes with a variety of guns, howitzers and mortars. Some details have been removed and others altered to make it more viable at this scale.

Perfect for playing the various editions of Epic, as objectives for Aeronautica Imperialis 2019 and as base toppers for Adeptus Titanicus 2018.

2021-02-27: Added a ZIP with improved details on the SPG carrier and shell stockpiles for decorating bases.

2021-04-23: Added a ZIP with hulls that have no details on the underside of the tracks and body, greatly reducing islands.

2022-01-30: Added new cannons. Removed redundant files and replaced zip files with a .3mf file.
~~~
