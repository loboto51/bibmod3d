A vile maggot from Dark Souls. Straight & posed models. Made for miniature tabletop games. Models are without base.

This model is based off of its appearance Dark Souls series:

I made these models myself - they are hand sculpted from basic cylinder shape in blender software.
- No custom brushes or library of tricks. yet.
- You can scale the model however you want.
- The models may be small, they are still chunky enough to be easily printed on low-end FDM machines without causing clogging issues (supports & brim required).
- You can use glossy finish to make it look more yucky.
