Description :

~~~
Thank you to MoonJam and Lord_Arner for many of the base parts!

Some Power Armoured Grey Knights for your Epic needs. These are scaled as 8mm marines like all of Moonjam's stuff. There are all the options needed for the current NetEA Developmental GK list. Everything for Strike Squads, Purgation Squads, Purifier Squads, Interceptor Squads, and even a little Crowe for good measure.

I have included supported .lys files for those who don't want the hassle of supporting them yourself. The supports are way more janky and not as nice as MoonJam's, but they do print. I have test printed them and they work.

Just a note, the painted models in the preview are from a previous version before purity seals were added. May the Emperor have mercy on your eyes when you are painting these.

These are largely based off of MoonJam's amazing Galactic Crusaders. I also took the helmets from Lord_Arner's Daybreak Strikers. There is also a Plaguebearer head for which I cannot find the link from jimjimjimmyjim in there so thanks to him as well. A few bits were my own design but the credit should really be theirs. Thingiverse is dumb and not letting me put their models in the remix section so links to models used are below.

https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:4277183
https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:4249300
https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:4599934

Added in a Banner remix by the fantastic Dave with parts from DavikDesigns, Gobothefraggle, and TZRock. Thanks for these!
~~~
