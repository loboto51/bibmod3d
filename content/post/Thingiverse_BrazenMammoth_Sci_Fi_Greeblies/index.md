~~~
I created these greeblies to add some visual interest to tabletop terrain, miniatures and all kinds of kit-bashing projects that need a little more spice. ;-)

There is a curved version of most of the parts fitting to a diameter of about 52mm. You might need to scale these parts up or down according to your needs! (I know - some curved STLs seem to have holes which I could not fix, but in the end they printed just fine without auto-repair.)

The robo legs in the pics will be a separate Thing on my profile as soon as I'm ready to publish them.

The light grey parts om the photos are parts from an early 2000s Games Workshop Leman Russ Battle Tank sprue.
~~~
