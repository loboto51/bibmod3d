Everyone loves tiny tanks! And everyone loves box launchers! Combine the two and double your pleasure!

Uses woddish's excellent original Tiny Bigger Tank Fancy APC model with a box launcher by me and a sensor turret remix from related parts from the S.P.R.U.E. Ground Plunderer.

You will need to print three parts for each tank: the box launcher (either in raised or lowered positions, the sensor turret, and a base hull.

Intended for resin printing. Examples printed on a Mars 2 at .02mm layer height.
