Description :

~~~
Leader and Father of the Fists. Useful for a diorama, or as part of a command squad.

Comes in five poses, with a choice of bare head shaven, or with mutton chops, or a helm!
For poses where he's not holding the sword, there's a couple options of having the sword planted in the ground, or a dead marine.

Considering this model is bigger than the usual 8mm infantry, He comes on a set of standoffs - the model can be painted, then cut off the standoffs to be glued to a base.

Thanks to Moonjammy and JimmyZimms for the base models, and the Legion Symbol is from Bumfluff.
The bare head I used as a base for this is from MrMcAngry
