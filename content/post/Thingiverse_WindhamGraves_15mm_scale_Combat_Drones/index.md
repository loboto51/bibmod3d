**Buy my time, Not my models.**

Near future combat (or not) drones at 1/100 (15mm). Designed to print on a filament printer so you can bend the fans to suit, I don't have a resin printer but I don't think resin will bend like that, I may be wrong. These are shown in the photograph next to a rebelminis 15mm operator in red, and a plastic soldier company 15mm WW2 German for scale. 

These are freakishly small to trim and assemble. The little dot attached to the back of the guns is there to fold over and act as a standoff from the drone body.

I would suggest just printing the "drones sampler" file as it makes 1 of each size and type of drone, all the bases, and 3 sets of weapons.
~~~
