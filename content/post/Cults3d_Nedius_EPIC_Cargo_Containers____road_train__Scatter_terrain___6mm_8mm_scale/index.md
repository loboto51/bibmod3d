Description :

~~~
The battlefields of the grim-dark future are scattered will the everyday materiel of the industrial and military complex required to process war on a galactic scale. 

This set offers a range of cargo containers, goods transport, trucks, tugs, trailers, cranes and crates to add a little more character to your battlefields and bases. 

These could be printed at 200% for 15mm gaming terrain, or, if you are ambitious, 400% for 28-32mm scale gaming. The models are sufficiently detailed to be printed at larger scale, yet simple and robust enough to print at their intended 6-8mm scale. 

This set contains 46 STLs, allowing you to customise your selection. It can be used to create cargo 'road trains', linking multiple trailers and cargo containers to create thematic barriers for your miniatures to take cover behind, or individual pieces to add character to the bases of larger miniatures. 

They would also make suitable 'toys' what could be added to 28-32mm scale models, adding a layer of horror and inhumanity to the devastation  surrounding your grim-dark characters. 

The crane is compatible with my other Epic tank turrets, so can be replaced with any of the turrets in this collection:
