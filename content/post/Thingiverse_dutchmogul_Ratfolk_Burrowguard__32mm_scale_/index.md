The first entry in our [Mischief of the Pale Spider](https://www.thingiverse.com/dutchmogul/collections/mischief-of-the-pale-spider) project (a ratfolk clan based on the bulkier/fluffier [Norway rat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brown_rat)). These models will require support to print. The models come un-based (because we like to put ours on clear acrylics), but you're free to add them to whatever bases you like, and this [short video will tell you how](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HGCwjUJVZ-0&t=1s) (it's super easy).

For those of you thinking about supporting us on [Patreon](https://www.patreon.com/illgottengames), our patrons get special access to our store releases as they come out (not to mention license options to sell printed versions of our designs).

Enjoy!
