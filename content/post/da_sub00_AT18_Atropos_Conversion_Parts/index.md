High Polygon Count Conversion files to turn an Adeptus Titanicus Cerastus Knight into an Knight-Atropos. 

Included Files:
- One Piece Carapace and Shoulders
- Mechanicus Style Shin Armour
- Singularity Cannon with integral Arm Mount
- Two Lascutter variants, to allow for more dynamic posing, with integral arm mounts

Please note: The Head from the demonstration photos is not included but will made available for free in the near future.

03/06/2020:
After some feedback I cleaned up the the STLs to decrease file size and increase ease of printing. 

15/08/2023:
Added Crotch and Thigh plates, ready scaled. Although there will be some work in scraping away the crotch plates on the plastic kits I figured it would be nice for people to have the option to do so. Thighs should fit easily enough although a second one will need to have be mirrored to do both sides (right hand side provided).
