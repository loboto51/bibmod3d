Designed these to be compatible with games such as Battlefleet Gothic, Dropfleet Commander, A Billion Suns, or Firestorm Armada. This kit contains the following items:

1) one individual Light Shuttle
2) one individual Heavy Shuttle
3) nine Light Shuttles on stems 
4) nine Heavy Shuttles on stems

The Light Shuttle is about 5mm long, 3.53mm wide, and 2.73mm tall. The Heavy Shuttle is 7.78mm long, 7,06mm wide, and 2.97mm tall.

If you need square bases to insert these in, I'd recommend getting [these from Vanguard Miniatures](https://vanguardminiatures.co.uk/index.php/product/20mm-square-plastic-bases-50/)
