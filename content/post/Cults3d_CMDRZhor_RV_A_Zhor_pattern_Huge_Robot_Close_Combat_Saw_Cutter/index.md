Description :

~~~
This is an enormous two-bladed close combat saw weapon for huge battle robots. It comes in four pieces - the actual saw part, an elbow joint, an upper arm piece, and the shoulder joint. You can simply superglue the parts together to achieve the handedness and posing you wish. The shoulder joint has a built-in half-socket for a 5mm disc magnet, and the saw is essentially symmetrical so it'll look just as good as a left or right arm (though you may want to keep which side you'll want to use it for in mind while you're planning your supports). The arm can be built as either a left or right arm.

As this is based on a handful of different (free) sources, I'm offering it for free:

The saw blade itself is from the Armor Bearer Saw by Arajar: https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3989916

And the actual arm is 40K Big Reaper Titan AT Style Chainfist by The_Titan_Manufactorium: https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/40k-big-reaper-titan-at-style-chainfist

Thank you for the awesome models!
