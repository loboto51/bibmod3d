A humble reworking of Moonjammy's excellent designs, optimised for stackable printing.

Love printing little dudes but hate having to get up every hour to scrape off your build plate, waste good resin in your wash station, and restart the print? Then here's the new easy-to-use stacking solution you've been waiting for! Stack small objects vertically to fit into your print schedule, or efficiently fill left over build plate volume when printing taller objects.

Including:
- **Galactic Crusader Infantry with Jump Jets** in 2egmented, 3oarding, and Pl4te armour
- Arranged in pre-built stacks of 20, 30 and 40.
- Raw components and a guide for creating custom stacks

**Scale and Sizing:**
The Galactic Crusaders in this pack have been resized to 105%.
This makes the average Galactic Crusader ~9.5mm tall (varying based on pose and armour style), and the base plugs 6.16x1.31mm.
