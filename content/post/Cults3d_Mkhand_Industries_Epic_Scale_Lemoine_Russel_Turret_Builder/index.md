A builder for Epic Scale Lemoine Russel Battle Tank Turrets, featuring all turret weapons and hatch styles allowing you to make the exact load outs your armoured units require. Some details have been removed and others altered to make them more viable at this scale.

Perfect for playing the various editions of Epic and as decorations for AT18/AI19.

Turrets have 2.5x1.25mm holes for magnetising them to hulls.
To use with plastic LIMP battle tank bodies, scale up by 125% in your slicer. Turrets are compatible with LIMP turret hatches when scaled up this way
