Description :

~~~
The biggest bioform the space bugs deploys in planetary battleground. Titan-sized and a direct neural link to the Norn-queens into the space bugs space fleet, this is the greatest menace you can face against the swarms.
For this model I´ve used a remix of all free models, mainly the Dominator space bug simp model from J4Roid in Thingiverse, modified to my own taste and hyperdetailed for 40K scale.
Is a really big model, I´ve tried to cut it to the smallest, but it will need a big printer to get it.

This model is remixed all from free public models:

- For base I used the free J4Roid Dominator space bug simp model model you can find in Thingiverse 
- Too from J4Roid (this guy is incredible, check his patreon too) I´ve used the brain bug you can fon in Cults Here , This Biovore proxy for the   main cannon and  the Hierodule for the scyties
- For the front legs I´ve used a model from Couch Commando Here in Cults
- The gorilla arms are from the Strokemyunicorns terrornid model
- And a lot of lesser parts for spikes etc., mainly free dinosaurs models from Cults too

This a very difficult and challenging project! only for jedi-level printers and modellers. The model is infernally spiked, take care!

You can find more info in my web at: inquisitorweb.mygamesonline.org

As allways, tips are welcome to improve my modelling tools. Enjoy!

Changelog:

1-11-22: Tail part repaired. Thanks to Reiner Zufall for finding a little printing error in one of the lower horns. Solved.
~~~
