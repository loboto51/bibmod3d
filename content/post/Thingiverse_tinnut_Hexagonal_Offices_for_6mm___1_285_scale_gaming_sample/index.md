Description :

~~~
This is a sample of a collection of hexagonal office buildings, for 6mm / 1:285 gaming - primarily Battletech and Epic 40k.  I'm selling the full set on [cults3d here](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/hexagonal-buildings-for-6mm-1-285-scale-gaming) to help justify to my significant other the time I spend working on these :)

They're designed to fit within the "standard" Battletech map hexes of 33mm flat edge to flat edge as is used in the current boxed sets. They also have a flat roof to allow Mech models to be placed on them.

The models are designed to be mostly supportless on an FDM printer and have printed fine on my Ender 3. I've intentionally kept the details low to speed up printing times and I find that if I print them at a higher speed that I'd normally use then I end up with a nice textured surface which is good for dry brushing.
~~~
