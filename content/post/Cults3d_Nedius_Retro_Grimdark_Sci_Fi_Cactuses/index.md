Description :

~~~
A pair of simple retro-style sci fi cactuses for tabletop RPGs and wargames. 

I designed these for a 15mm scale project I'm working on, for which I print them at 50% scale. These are scaled to be 40mm and 60mm (ish) tall when printed at 100%, so would work for 28mm-32mm scale miniatures. However, they are intentionally simple and chunky, both to have they retro feel and to work when scaled down. They should work at anything down to 25% scale, if you fancied some spikey plants for your 8mm games!

Great for retro themed grim dark scifi terrain, for games such as 40k, SW Legions, etc.

Please see my other models and consider donating. 

Thanks!
