Description :

~~~
Available for deployment!

Even if some deride it for it's seeming lack of firepower, heavy mortar carrier forms a vital part of many an imperial army. Quick and accurate it can launch prompt and precise bombardments in close cooperation with frontline troops. 

The same chassis is also utilized for a family of recon and command vehicles. These open-topped vehicles make great use of chassis' powerful engines to perform reconnaissance and patrol tasks.

These stls allow you to add heavy mortar carrier, recon tank or a command vehicle to your tiny (around 8mm) grimdark forces. Unlike previous releases this one designed for a track units (along with some hull details) to be printed as separate parts to improve printing and allow for addition of more variants down the line. So far some variants are included with track guards and variants of vehicle hulls. Recon and command tanks can be printed with their interior covered with tarp if you are not feeling like painting the interiors. Command tank includes both heavy flamethrower and heavy gyrojet as it's main weapons. In addition a mine clearing device and two variants of sideskirt armour are included for further customization. I also plan on adding some more cosmetic variants and accessories for these in the future. 

Finally, apologies for a long wait, hopefully I can pick up pace going forwards.

UPDATE 11.04.2024 - fixed undersides of the trackunits to sit flat added a dozer blade to accessories 

UPDATE 05.11.2024 - fixed an issue with tracks that failed to adhere to the the trackunit under some circumstances as pointed out to me.

UPDATE 15.03.2025 - reworked track units in preparation for the new releases on this chassis to hopefully solve the issues for good. Added a new version with quarter track guards + versions without decorations on hatch for those pesky renegades while I was at it
~~~
