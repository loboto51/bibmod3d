Description :

~~~
The Warmaster titan model kit from Games Workshop comes with only two weapon mounts for the shoulder weapons, but there are five pairs of weapons that could use it. I wanted to magnetize these weapons and keep this modular for WYSIWYG gaming. So I made a spacer block that takes the place of the original part and permits me to easily do what I want.

Update: increased the weapon slot by 0.5 mm and the recess for the nub by 0.25 mm, each in both dimensions. This means the plastic weapon bits fit snug, but without any need to push them hard into the fitting.
~~~
