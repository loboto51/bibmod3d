Description :

~~~
The Factorio locomotive made into a keychain! each piece is ~33mm long with the holes in the center being 2mm across

I used 7 strand steel beading wire. to thread through the pieces then leaving a little under the length of the last wagon extra to loop back into opposite holes. I jammed a piece of copper wire in to keep it from pulling back out and put a nice gob of super glue on the back to keep it from pulling out.

The length is basically as long as you want it to be. 

I added the Raw models without holes for those making minis (the bottom isnt totally flat)
~~~
