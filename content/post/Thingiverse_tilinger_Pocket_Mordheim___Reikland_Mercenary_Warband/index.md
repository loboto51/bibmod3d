Description :

~~~
Besides Warmaster, Mordheim was the other Specialist Games wargame I always wanted to sink into. I had the chance to play a few games with my club back then, but the interest faded away in the group. I though it would be interesting to check whether it works in 10mm so I quickly assembled a Reiklander mercenary warband for testing purposes. I guess these will not be that useful for others, the minis are quite specific, but at lest they can be useful if I ever want to do an empire Warmaster army.

Here is the roster:
Captain - sword, pistol (85)
Champion - halberd, bow (55)
Champion - halberd (45)
Youngblood - spear, bow (35)
Youngblood - spear, bow (35)
4 Marksman - crossbow (4x50)
1 Swordsmen - sword (45)
