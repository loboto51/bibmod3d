Alloteu (https://cults3d.com/en/users/Alloteu/3d-models) is the original designer of Lawdogs.  Evilamericorp (https://cults3d.com/en/users/evilamericorp/3d-models) & Dimkaf (https://cults3d.com/en/users/dimkaf/3d-models) took his models, reposed them, and added additional bits to create alternate models.

I just took Evilamericorp's and Dimkaf's creations, removed the non-Lawdog stuff and collected them together so you could quickly access additional Lawdog models.

Note these guys have no heads!  But I've included 3 of Alloteu's Lawdog Heads (with a ball, cylinder, or hole at the bottom) for you to use.
