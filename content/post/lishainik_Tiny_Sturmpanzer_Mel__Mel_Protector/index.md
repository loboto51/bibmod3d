- trench rails
- roof mounted searchlight 

Furthermore our decoration team furnished optional imperial insignia to be affixed on prominent locations of your tank.

Autocannons, Laser Cannons, Heavy Gyrojets and a mighty Siege Howitzer are provided for sponson and hull mounts. The durable casemate  can mount either a powerful cannon, long barreled tank killer cannon or a dual laser cannons. 

In addition to that a specialized Protector variant is provided fully festooned with anti personnel weaponry for those hectic Mondays.

Furthermore all roof hatches are rendered fully operational so that you can always fulfil your urge to catch that fragrant smell of phosgene in the air.

Sturmpanzer Mel represents an exciting new chassis to be added to our tiny tank offering and is perfect for vehicular combat in 6 to 8mm scales!
