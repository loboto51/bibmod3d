Description :

~~~
Stroganoff made some beautiful Leman Russ models in 6mm for games like Epic. That's great and all, but a 10 Russ company has 9 Main Battle Tanks, and one Vanquisher in NetEA. And then there's the Demolishers you can attach in 3s. What's a gamer to do?

Make them! 

Here you will find some variant turrets for Leman Russ chassis tanks. Demolisher, Vanquisher, Punisher, Conqueror, Executioner, Eradicator, Exterminator (come on GeeDubs, those names are just confusing) The Exterminator could double as an Annihilator. 

There are two Punisher turrets. A 6 barrel and a 4 barrel. The 4 prints cleaner, but the 6 did not feel as flimsy to me. So your milage may vary there. Could have been my post-processing.

These have been printed on an SLA printer. Some of these items are not going to print well on an FDM printer. The Punisher and Exterminator come to mind. I might edit the Eradicator a little as it has little supports on the side which might be too thin/separated from the main barrel. It won't really affect printing, but might not form well in the process. 
~~~
