Description :

~~~
These are my versions of the Nova and Gladius Frigates from that no longer available tabletop game of epic space battles in the Gothic Sector. I have patterned these after those found on the electronic game of the same name. I have tried to get the scale correct and I hope they are close. I really enjoyed making them and they look awesome printed!

All of them were test printed on an FDM printer at .08 layer height with a .4 nozzle. Angle them up at 45 degrees and be careful while removing supports.

I have placed a starter 3mm hole in the bottoms. I use a 1/8 drill bit and 1/8 styrene rod.. but use what you would like.

These were made from scratch using base primitives.
~~~
