Description :

~~~
This is a mix of Italianmoose's (https://www.thingiverse.com/Italianmoose/about) and Corvus Obscurus's (https://www.thingiverse.com/Corvus_Obscurus/about) eldar cruisers, to create a simple to assemble Void Stalker. The hull is a mishmash of Italianmoose's cruiser, while the wings are from Corvus's light cruiser.

This is modeled at about 80% of what *I* think the size should be. Although I can't tell how big the original was because I don't have one, this model gets close I'm guessing.

The upper and lower wings are halves, print two of each file. They are identical on each half as well as the left/right wing, so gluing them together for filament prints won't be hard. They fit on the model at a right-angel block, so again, easy assembly.
~~~
