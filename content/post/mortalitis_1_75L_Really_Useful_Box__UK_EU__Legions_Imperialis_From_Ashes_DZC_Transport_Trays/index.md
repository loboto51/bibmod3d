Description :

~~~
Trays are designed to be used with 1.75L Really Useful Boxes (UK/EU) to maximise the space inside when carrying minis for smaller scale games like Legions Imperialis, From Ashes or Dropzone Commander. Simply apply some ferrous rubber sheet to the floor of the tray and then you can stack multiple layers of magnetised minis inside a single RUB.

Each 1.75L RUB has approx 60mm of inner height so the trays are used in the following combinations.

3x 20mm Trays
2x 30mm Trays
1x 40mm Tray + 1x 20mm Tray

20mm trays are good for Legions Imperialis infantry minis, rhinos/smaller vehicles
30mm trays are good for most other vehicles and From Ashes (15mm scale) infantry
40mm trays are good for the larger minis like Legions Imperialis automata

Based on the design by JTSWargaming (https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/legions-imperialis-storage-tray-inserts-for-4l-really-useful-boxes). His designs are only good for the US version of 4L RUB, the UK/EU 4L is a bit wider  so it is incompatible. I also found these to be a little too big to fit on my printer as I needed bed adhesion support to avoid lifting.
~~~
