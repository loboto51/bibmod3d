Noticed the backpack was wrong on SebTheis's Nuka Girl and the heels were weird so after starting remixing her with a new backpack I got a bit carried away and replaced her legs with shapelier ones, redid a hand, remade the collar from scratch, replaced her face, redid the suit sockets, and the buckles and tried to give her a few more curves and sharper clothes, though damn difficult in meshmixer. Hope you all like her, hope those heels print without breaking.

V2 With the help of SebTheis the clothes have had their details sharpened, then I cut her in half to get the shirt bottom very sharp, did some tweaks to her back, also redid the most of the belt and redid the hoops over the belt. I've also scaled her feet slightly bigger so that it has a better chance of being printable.
~~~
