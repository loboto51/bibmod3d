~~~
A 1/5th resizing of all previously released M'khand Pattern Heavy Tank Turret & Weapons files, plus large speakers, decals and a variety of commander's hatch. Ideal for uparming epic scale lemoine russel battle tanks, or fitting onto epic scale fortified anti-air emplacements or epic scale M'khand Pattern HDC Heavy Tanks.

Some details have been altered or removed to ensure proper printing at this size. 3.5x1.25mm holes are included for magnetising turrets to tank hulls. 

The zip includes the meshmixer fils which can have any other epic scale lemoine russel variants placed in to maximise the use of said parts. Simply Shift+Click the pieces you want to use and select export under the FILE dropdown menu. Alternatively, parts can be assembled via 3dBuilder or another software of your choice. Exported pieces are then ready for repair in netfab/3dbuilder/prusaslicer and slicing.
~~~
