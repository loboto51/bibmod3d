The most (in)famous heroes of the Old World arrive to reinforce (for a price, of course) your small (and not evil) army!

To model Felix I used the Human Ranger created by Valandar, I changed his boots and added the sword modeled by Eskultura (whose details are not seen at this scale but it is an excellent resource to take into account if you want to create your own Felix Jaeger ). That simple, fast and beautiful, like Felix himself.

Gotrek was another story. There are few and not very good free models of dwarf slayers and none really convinced me, so I had no choice but to take what I found and cut, paste, modify, remodel, cut again, paste over what was pasted, add pieces and pray to Grimnir and Valaya. The result, I must say, left me quite satisfied :)

As a base I used the Dwarf Berserker created by JD-SCI-FY, the axe is also from Eskultura, and little else.

I hope you enjoy them!


- Human Ranger: https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/valandar-e616
- Axe and sword: https://cults3d.com/en/users/EsKultura/3d-models
- Dwarf Berserker: https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/kraggi-doom-wake-dwarf-berserker
~~~
