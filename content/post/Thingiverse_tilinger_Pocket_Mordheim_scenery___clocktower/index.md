~~~
This is a ruined cloctower for 10mm Mordheim, based on the Uhrturm of Graz. The front clock can be used as turn tracker. This time my kids also helped a bit, some of the wood texture was made by them. Also the idea of adding a nest with a bird came from my son. I also added some ladders, they will be needed to reach the top floors more easy.

Additional sources used:
https://www.myminifactory.com/object/3d-print-coat-of-arms-stemmi-78442
https://www.printables.com/model/649322-graz-clocktower-wall-clock?lang=en

You might be able to use it for normal Mordhiem as well, it needs to printed at x2,54 size. The detail might not be that great though, and you will need a lot larger printing area.
~~~
