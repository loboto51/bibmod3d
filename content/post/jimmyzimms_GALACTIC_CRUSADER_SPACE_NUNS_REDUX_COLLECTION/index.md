Description :

~~~
Rebuild of my previous Battle Sisters from the ground up to have more source correct features (see https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:5153040 and https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/space-nuns-combined-collection for previous content).

Contains tactical sisters with Bolters, Flamers, and Melta in 5 poses.

Heavy Weapons with Heavy Bolter, Heavy Flamer, Multimelta support sisters.

Seraphim in 5 poses with bolt pistols or hand flamers. 2 Sister Superior leaders.
 
All models come in helmets and helmetless versions.

Blender source included!

Includes parts rebuilt based on parts originally from Rhimlock, Base_Department, and Moonjammy
