Description :

~~~
Remixed from Lo_Copio_too's remix of JonPVogel's paint holder! I loved the design but I don't have a permanent space to keep my paints so I needed a solution that was portable. I also have a few Citadel paints which don't fit in the standard rack. So I designed another rack that fits 12 Citadel bottles and 5 of the droppers on the bottom. It's a pretty hasty and rough design but it does the job. I also cut the Vallejo model to accept a handle. You may have to do a little bit of cutting to get it to fit but it should be a nice tight fit. You could potentially print two Vallejo or two Citadel if you don't need one of each. Thanks to both of the original creators for the files! No supports needed for any of the files, as long as you can do a 1.5" span.
~~~
