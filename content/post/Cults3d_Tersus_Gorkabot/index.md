FOR BEST QUALITY PRINT, PLEASE READ ALL(!) OF BELOW:





To print the Gorkabot, you need time or a resin printer ;-)


I REALLY recommend a layer high of 0.075 or lower for all parts to get the quality I intended.


Please see the pics, those are all printed in FDM (<=0,075mm)!





I tried to keep the need of tree supports low, but I highly recommend it to use them - make sure to set the tree support z-distance to a multiple of 2 (layer hight) for optimal removal (keep in mind: layer hight 0.05 to 0.075(!) )

"GorkaBot_Belly.stl" prints without tree support! Just use regular supports.

The doors should be printed without supports with a brim (see Cura screenshot)

The legs are unique, there is an individual model for the right / left leg. Those are mirrorable / interchangeable if you want.
