Description :

~~~
Let's face it:  Even in the far future, people are still obsessed with efficiency, doing more with less.  So it is with maintaining the Imperious Leader's far-flung dominion, and the most efficient method of moving stuff is still the train.  And when you have enemies, those trains make sweet, juicy targets, necessitating adding arms and armor to repel the bad guys.

This set includes not only nine different train cars, but compatible track segments.  The various train cars have round guide lugs on the underside that serve to keep the cars centered on the tracks.

The GothiTech Armored Train includes:

- Armed Locomotive
- Flatbed Car
- Boxcar
- Tanker Car
- Passenger Car
- Nuclear Fuel Car
- Artillery Car (cannon prints separately and can be mounted up for firing or down for travel)
- Troop Car (includes mounting points for two turrets)
- Air Defense Car (includes two antiaircraft missile turrets)
- Five Track Segments (Straight Single, Curved Single, Y-Split, Straight Double, and Curved Double)
~~~
