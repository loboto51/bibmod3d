Description :

~~~
Rumbling forward on heavy tracks, the tanks of the Imperial Army are capable of decimating enemy forces with overwhelming firepower. The versatile main battle tank is capable of being adapted to feature a wide range of alternate patterns - support vehicles capable of hauling damaged vehicles or artillery, hulls with sponsons, dozer blades and ram bars.

This is a set of tank hulls featuring 8 base hull designs - a Destroyer hull, Thunderer hull, Flamer hull, laser cannon hull, heavy bolt hull, automatic cannon hull, and two support variant hulls. Each of these come with bulldozer blade and ram bar options. Each of the standard tank hull designs also comes with options for two different sponson designs (square and inset versions) , with holes for magnetisation. Included are also two turret options, to create complete tanks.

These have been made to be compatible with 8mm scale main line battle tanks of the Imperial Army in a recently released 8mm grim-dark future wargame. They feature a magnet hole, and a peg compatible with existing plastic model turrets. 


I've priced this cheaply, but priced it as a reflection of the time I took to create such an extensive set. I deeply appreciate all those who support and encourage my work by buying any of my paid designs.

Please see my other items for a free sample of this set, and a range of other 8mm scale and other wargames and hobby models, including a set of compatible turret options for these tanks. I am an enthusiastic amateur designer, self and video-tutorial taught, so am always open to constructive feedback.
