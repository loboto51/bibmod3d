Description :

~~~
An improved pattern of the M'khand Pattern Lemoine Russel Battle Tank, with improved suspension and internal layout offering more crew comfort and higher accuracy when firing on the move. Comes with all turret coxial and sponson weapon options  offered at larger scales for maximum customisation.

Completed bodies, turrets and middles have 2.5x1.25mm holes for magnets. Compatible with all epic scale M'khand-pattern Lemoine Russel parts and variants. Comes with a variety of decals and effects.

The zip includes all my parts and meshmixer files. With Meshmixer (free program from Autodesk), simply Shift+Click the pieces you want to use and select export under the FILE dropdown menu. Alternatively, parts can be assembled via 3dBuilder, Blender or another software of your choice. Exported pieces are then ready for repair in netfab/3dbuilder/prusaslicer and slicing.

For best results when printing destroyed turrets, include a closed hatch  with the turret, weapon and smoke to prevent a small pocket of unprinted resin.

__For printing for LIMP/AT18, increase scale to 125% when slicing__

2023-12-03: Added new options for side weapons and a builder with all my other lemoine russel variants in it.

2023-12-07: Added adapters to fit the turrets to plastic LIMP hulls. Combine with other wanted parts before scaling up 125%

2023-12-07: Split hull weapon mounts from the hulls. Added new hull weapon options. Removed the old redundant builder file.

2024-01-14: Added coronal-designations of the three patterns of turret as well as both a turret-mounted heat ray and heavy flamethrower.

2024-08-24: Added internal side sponsons.
