12mm model (bit) to replace the bridge sculpture on Imperial battleships in the classic tabletop wargame Battlefleet Gothic. The style and scale would also be appropriate for 6mm epic / 8mm AT18 scale scenery or statuary on Imperial buildings in the tabletop wargames Space Marine / Adeptus Titanicus. This figure is made in 10 different poses: wings open / closed, sword down / raised (R/L), with / without shield.
