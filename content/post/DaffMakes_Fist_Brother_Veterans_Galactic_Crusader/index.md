Description :

~~~
Honoured veterans of the Fist Legion, for both Crusading and protecting the mothership!

Two Armour Types, Boarding and Plate, Customised for the fist legion
9 Poses. Options for Radio and a Banner.
One pose, with the shield planted on the floor only worked with the bigger shields, since the other is smaller.
Weapon options for Chainsword, Power Sword, Power Axe and Power fist,
Three shield types, one for crusaders, one for mothership protectors, and a bladguard shield.
options for robes and tabards too, for that crusader fashion

Check out the "_standoffs" version. The models' feet are offset from the plug, allowing you to use the plug as a stand when painting, and then slice or snip off the small cylinders to allow you to glue the feet directly onto the base, for a seamless finish.

Thanks to Moonjammy, DangitBlobby and JimmyZimms for the base models, the Ball Bearing Armour and Beaky Armour, on which this is based.
A BIG thanks to LSTAR, who helped me with the export script for these guys. I wouldn't be able to put out the variety I have without it!
