Description :

~~~
A modular/stackable tower structure to support the GW MANUFACTORUM IMPERIAL crane. (Epic/Legions Imperial/Adeptus Titanicus scale)
Multiple tower segments can be stacked on top of each other to create towers of various heights. Each segment includes pins on top & holes on the bottom that slot together for ease of assembly & disassembly. Top piece has larger projecting pins that slot into underside of the GW crane support frame/platform.
Segments also have a sloped ladder & platform that form a continuous spiral ladder when segments are stacked rotated 90degrees to the segment above/below
~~~
