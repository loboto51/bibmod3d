A set of modular walls and roofs intended to make a wide variety of 8mm scale buildings - warehouses, hangars, worker barracks, etc. Designed in a simple, pre-fab style intended to give a look of quickly churned out industrial buildings. 

Please bear in mind that due to the nature of the pieces and their fit, some sanding/filing will almost always be necessary for proper fit. 

Set includes 10 different mid-height walls, 7 high (double the size of mid), 5 roof, some support pieces for joining the straight sections and one double wide section each of mid and high wall. 

I`ve test printed on Anycubic Photon Mono 2 at 35 micron layer height. Results are on the photos. 

I strongly suggest first trying those free test pieces to see if you can get acceptable results from your printer:
