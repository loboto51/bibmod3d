Description :

~~~
A free upgrade kit for your smaller knight models, released along with the Project Abby conversion kit found here...

https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/project-abby-conversion-kit-for-naughty-knights

Included in this kit are...

-FOUR nasty blades, including three chain weapons and a cleaner-looking power weapon.

-FIVE claws, including a pincer with dual saws.

-Three triple-missile launchers in vertical, horizontal, and triangular configurations.  Two poses are offered: straight ahead, and tilted back for launch.

-UPDATE: The missile launchers were a little big for my taste, so I have included 12 new files (supported and clean) for launchers reduced about 15%.  Either can be used.  Also, if you're using the mounting peg, some sanding/filing may be necessary, especially after paint.

-Three main weapon bodies, with three degrees of tilt.

-Two forearm weapons extensions.

-Five Suturus Pattern upper arms at various fixed angles.  The upper arms twist-lock to fit with factory kits.

Note: This upgrade kit requires a small amount of hobby time and effort.
I recommend you magnetize the upper arms at the elbow flat with a 8mm-wide diameter x 3mm- or 2mm-thick magnet.
I would then glue steel washers to the top of the main weapon body.

Thanks!

Best, JBR

Please use these files and their resulting prints for private use only, unless you have secured a production license. Gracias.

Supported and Clean versions of all files are in the zipped folders.

All .stl files optimized for printing on resin 3d printers.
~~~
