10mm minotaur heavy infantry for warmaster revolution (counts-as ogre kingdoms ironguts)

The minotaurs come from here: https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/labyrinth-guardians

I had some difficulty with these as the heavily armored minotaur stls I found didn't really fit in the warhammer universe's savage beast feel of beastmen.
