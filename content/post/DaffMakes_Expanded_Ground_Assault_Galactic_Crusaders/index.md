Description :

~~~
Ground-Based Close Combat Galactic Crusaders.

Five Armour Types, Segmented, Boarding, Plate, Ball-Bearing and Nu-Beaky.
Ten Poses.
Weapon options for Chainaxe, Chainswords, Two types of Claws and Dual Claws, a Combat Knife, Power Fist, Power Sword, and a Hammer!
Options for pistols include a Bolt, Plasma and Heat Pistol.

Check out the "_standoffs" version. The models' feet are offset from the plug, allowing you to use the plug as a stand when painting, and then slice or snip off the small cylinders to allow you to glue the feet directly onto the base, for a seamless finish.

Thanks to Moonjammy, DangitBlobby and JimmyZimms for the base models, the Ball Bearing Armour and Beaky Armour, on which this is based.
A BIG thanks to LSTAR, who helped me with the export script for these guys. I wouldn't be able to put out the variety I have without it!
