Description :

~~~
This is sample from a larger collection of sci-fi office buildings, for 6mm / 1:285 gaming - primarily Battletech but also Epic 40k and other games at that scale, that I sell over [here on cults3d](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/sci-fi-offices-for-6mm-1-285-scale-gaming) for not a lot.  I sell them to help justify to my significant other the time I spend working on these :)

The buildings are combinations of tower blocks that equate to level 1, 2 and 3 within Battletech.

They're designed to fit entirely within the "standard" Battletech map hexes of 33mm flat edge to flat edge as is used in the current boxed sets. They also have a roof designed to allow Mech models to be placed on them.

The models are designed to be mostly supportless on an FDM printer and have printed fine on my Ender 3.

Edit Jan-2022: Updated the models to the fixed versions.
~~~
