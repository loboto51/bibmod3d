A set of trench pieces scaled for epic/6-8mm. This set is designed to be used for gameplay purposes, so the trenches are large enough to fit 20mm wide bases in them easily.

Use them to represent entrenchments and fortifications. The source file is also included so you can also make your own adjustments or your own trenches.

Included - 

2 straight trench pieces
1 trench exit ramp piece
1 trench with a dugout
1 T-junction piece
1 corner piece
1 slanted piece
1 trench with a vision slit
2 "pits", 1 with a raised platform
1 Blender file with all pieces

Enjoy! 

*Update* It was reported that some pieces weren't flat so were a little harder to print flat to the base, so I endeavoured to make all pieces flat.
