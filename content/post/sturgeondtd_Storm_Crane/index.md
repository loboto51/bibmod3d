Description :

~~~
My own take on a Super Heavy aerial transport for Space Marines dubbed the Storm Crane. Sized to be used in Epic/Legion Imperialis games.

Unique in its role, Storm Cranes are able to deliver a variety of assets to the front. From 10 fully embarked rhinos, or 4 kratos heavy battle tanks or any other armored detachment, Storm Cranes are able to bring full detachments of armor needed on the battle field. Protected by heavy plating and a pair of void shields this flyer can withstand any punishment short of anti-titan weapons. Ensuring its cargo successfully arrives where it is needed. Each Strom Crane is armed with two audax heavy bolter arrays and two titan sized havoc launchers making it a formidable assault craft that can break through enemy lines. A truly armored work horse, Space Marine legions relied heavily on these vehicles throughout the crusade and into the Heresy.


While I designed most of this using 3D Builder, I did borrow some choice parts from free kits.
Winglets/Audax Turrets from MKHand Industries
Thruster Turbine and Exhaust  from IronMaster
Titan Hatch and Void Shields from Danny_Cyanide
~~~
