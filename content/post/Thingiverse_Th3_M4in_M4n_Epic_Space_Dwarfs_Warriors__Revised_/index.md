~~~
This are Epic Size Squat Warriors designed for the Grindel Sronghold army list that you can find here: https://tacticalwargames.net/taccmd/viewtopic.php?f=134&t=33281&sid=422418b4bea515739e1aef8d862588e5

I updated of my previuous Squat Warriors. I revised the models and made some small, yet important changes:

First i simplified the models. Theres no point to print high detailed models because at Epic scale they simply wont be noticed or even printed. Second, i added supports, wich were pretty much needed in some of the models.

Also i added a warrior equiped with a Missile Launcher, because the rules indicate that one of each two units will be equiped wiht this and i wanto to see it on the gameboard. The sargeants (the guys with axes) are entirelly for aestetic pruposes.
