~~~
Do It Yourself (DIY) terrain scenery for WH40k. This "Knight's Gate" is 150mm X 202mm / 5.9" X 7.95" (Width and Height). It can be affixed on a wall and closed, or you can use both sides to create a gate that opens.

The inspiration for these doors has been some old GW art and a commission that required the incorporation of imperial Knight Titan themes.

A "sibling door" can be found in my other posts under:
https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:5669332

Other DIY pieces and greebles can be found here:
https://www.thingiverse.com/vontragg/collections/42607514/things

I have used a number of shields that I found all over the place. If I have failed to give attribution, please contact me and I will be happy to correct any omissions.
