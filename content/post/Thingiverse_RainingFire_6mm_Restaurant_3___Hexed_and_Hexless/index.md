Description :

~~~
A nice little tea shop for anywhere in the universe, this building is designed to be 3D printed as terrain for 6mm tabletop gaming. The top of the building is large and flat enough to support a unit placed on top. It is 1 level tall if you're playing a specific mecha tabletop game.

There are four files. One has the building on hexes, another is hexless so that you can print the one that fits your playstyle, and there are two more versions that have the text removed from the front of the building. The versions with text may only look good when printed in resin, so if you print with FDM you may prefer to print the NoText files. In that case, you can paint a name on the front, print off a logo to attach, or leave it blank.

Notable features:
-Nice exterior design that evokes a Japanese feel.
-Detailed design including doors, windows, a ladder to the roof, and a detailed roof with greebles.

The model units are in mm and it is scaled such that the hexes are 33mm from flat-to-flat, which is perfect for modern hex maps using 33mm hexes. You can always resize it if your hexes are slightly smaller/larger.

Please post pictures and leave feedback if you make this yourself! I'd love to see prints and use the feedback to adjust overhangs, detail levels, etc. on this model and future ones as well.
~~~
