Description :

~~~
A handle for painting tabletop miniatures, that can hold any model with a 32 mm or 40 mm base plate.

The grip is ergonomic and makes handling the model a lot easier. The two clamps are designed such that the model fits snuggly and won't move or budge while painting.

The only thing you need to add is a small rubber band that pulls the clamps together, in my case I used a cheap hair elastic.

Have fun painting!
~~~
