Provides a version of Woddish's excellent Tiny Tank Fancy APC and related things that have been sliced into major parts that are enabled to be quickly swapped and edited for customization by changing visibility of components between different options for hatches, side doors, symbols/icons, and crew. The eventual end game would be the ability to build any Mars based Monoceros chassis vehicle with this tool.

This is intended for designers and modelers to product their own faction customized vehicles from in a standardized and high quality form.
