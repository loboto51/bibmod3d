~~~
Suitable in size and armament to be a stand in for an Inquisition Cruiser in the classic tabletop wargame Battlefleet Gothic. This is mostly a re-mix of Italianmoose sculpts; the hull based on the Italianmoose Imperial cruiser, both Italianmoose Chaos cruiser bridge versions are mixed together, the Italianmoose battle barge underbridge is included in the superstructure and that's also where the wings and gun sections come from, the prow is from the Italianmoose strike cruiser. The engines are built from scratch sculpt by me.

I am not affiliated with GW in any way. Please don't reupload this on Shapeways and the like for commercial use (though you're welcome to upload them to get them printed that way for yourself).
