Additional turrets for Epic Scale Lemoine Russel Battle Tanks, fitted with extra communication and targetting equipment as befits formation commanders and ace crews. Some details have been removed and others altered to make them more viable at this scale.

Perfect for playing the various editions of Epic and as base toppers for Adeptus Titanicus 2018.

Turrets and hulls have 2.5x1.25mm holes for magnetising them to the bodies.
