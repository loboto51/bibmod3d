Description :

~~~
This cephalopod inspired robot is a ghoulish spektor that haunts archeotech ruins.

It is a remix of Akewea's Sci-Fi Sphere Drone (https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:5266420) and Franklima260's Mini Servo Arm (https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/sci-fi-sphere-drone)

The arms are fully posable with ball & socket joints at the shoulder, elbow, and wrist.

Since the attachment point to the base is pretty small, I've included 4 additional versions of the tentacles with a rod cutout for pinning of the following sizes
- 1mm brass rod (1.1mm hole)
- 1.5mm brass rod (1.6mm hole)
- 0.8mm thin paperclip (0.9mm hole)
- 1.15mm thick paperclip (1.25mm hole)
~~~
