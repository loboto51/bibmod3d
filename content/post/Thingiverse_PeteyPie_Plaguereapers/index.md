Hello this is my first proper upload of anything here!

Been needing Epic Scale Plaguereapers from the Chaos God of Decay for a while, and after converting a few, I got into 3D printing, so this was my first major project. I did use a lot of parts from other builds, so there's a list at the bottom for that. I certainly did not build these things from scratch, I just stuck things together and mutated them, so I can take no credit for the finished designs.

Not all of these are perfect, like I say this is my first real go at designing things. Some will be better printed out as resin, and some in finer detail than what I could manage on my humble Monoprice printer. But they should certainly do the trick on the battlefield. 

You'll see some of these have the main cannons attached as one piece, where there are separate cannons to chose from for others. Please feel free to fiddle and adjust these how you see fit.
