Description :

~~~
A true-scale 6mm version of the M'khand Pattern Anti-Air Artillery weapon system. Comes with a variety of long-barreled autocannons and both SSM & SAM missiles. Some details have been removed and others altered to make it more viable at this scale.

Turret and turret ring have a 3.5x1.25mm hole for magnets, but 2x1mm magnets are sufficient to keep them together.

Perfect for playing the various editions of Epic, surface flak in Aeronautica Imperialis and as base toppers for Adeptus Titanicus 2018.

2021-02-27: Added updated versions with more details.

2021-04-23: Added a ZIP with a hull that has no details on the underside of the tracks, greatly reducing islands.
~~~
